Love Is Blind star Carlton Morton once got into HUGE fight on camera with Kenya Moore on RHOA Th

Publish date: 2024-08-14

LOVE is Blind star Carlton Morton got into a huge on-camera fight with Real Housewives of Atlanta star Kenya Moore while working as Cynthia Bailey’s assistant.

Carlton appeared on the season five premiere of RHOA in 2012, years before he joined Netflix series Love is Blind to find his wife.

On RHOA, Cynthia had an audition for models and asked Kenya to help pick one for a magazine shoot.

When one woman walked in a bikini for the judges, Kenya said: “I’m not trying to be the mean girl, but why are you here? I’m just being truthful.”

When a second model took the stage for the audition in a barely-there black bikini, Kenya made a “strip club” comment and called her out for not shaving.

Carlton confronted Kenya for her harsh words.

He said: “I just think there is a respectful way… some of them are coming here … this is their first time modeling.”

She fired back: “You need to stop. Excuse me. Who are you and why are you talking to me?”

Carlton yelled back: “I work for the Bailey Agency. Who are you because I’ve never met you.”

Kenya snapped: “B***h please!

Carlton then said: “B***h?! You don’t see yourself. I’m not holding up a mirror. Don’t be disrespectful towards me.”

Kenya then called her security guard to escort Carlton away from her.

On Love is Blind, Carlton, 34, proposed to Diamond Jack, 28, after talking in their pods for days without meeting each other.

But while vacationing in Mexico, Carlton, 34, revealed he is bisexual.

Diamond felt Carlton lied to her because he did not reveal the information before he proposed.

She said in her confessional: “I felt blindsided. Was this really true love? Was this real? I love Carlton. I hope it is going to be okay. It’s going to work itself out. I need to talk to him.”

When they met at the pool, she said to him: “I feel like I kept it real with you since day one. I personally feel you left out who you really are. I feel like you kind of played the experiment in a way.”

He responded: “I wanted you to get to know me for me... In the beginning, you would pre-judge me. If I would’ve told you on the first date, would you have really given me a chance?”

She said: “I’d rather you had told me upfront. I would have been able to make a better decision.”

Carlton replied: “Whatever. Show your true colors. I showed mine.”

Diamond fired back: “You waited literally four weeks from our wedding to show your true colors.”

She then threw a drink in his face.
