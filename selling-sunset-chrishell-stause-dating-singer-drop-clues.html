Selling Sunset fans think Chrishell Stause, 40, is dating singer, 27, after both stars drop 'clues'

Publish date: 2024-05-19

CHRISHELL Stause has fans questioning whether she's dating Australian musician G Flip after a photo of the pair holding hands surfaced.

The Selling Sunset star has bonded with the singer since her split from broker and co-star Jason Oppenheimer late last year.

Recent pics have ignited chatter that Chrishell and G Flip - real name Georgia Claire Flipo - are more than friends.

A Reddit user shared a snap of the pair from behind, holding hands while at a concert in Denver, Colorado.

The Netflix star wore low-rise blue jeans and a leather jacket with her new short brunette hair flowing down.

Her rumored lover opted for gray pants, a silver belt, a red jacket, and a black baseball cap.

The duo have often sent flirty messages to one another on social media, making fans wonder if they are romantically involved.

NEW ROMANCE?

Another hint that the two are dating arose on Chrishell's Instagram Stories, where the TV personality revealed she was taking a trip to G Flip's native Australia.

A TikToker shared the reality star's post, pointing out the country's connection to her rumored partner.

"Peeps I'm finally checking Australia off my bucket list!" Chrishell wrote. "What is the best hotel in Sydney - Surrey Hills area?" she added.

A fan then asked the star if she was stopping in the city of Melbourne, to which the 40-year-old responded: "Yes! But already have that one sorted."

The social media user noted that Melbourne is where G Flip lives, speculating that Chrishell will be staying with them during her stay.

The user also shared a screenshot from the TV star's Instagram Stories of Chrishell and G Flip's date night the night before the season five premiere of Selling Sunset.

The latest season focuses on Chrishell's brief romance with real estate mogul Jason, 44, before their split.

The former couple was first linked in the summer of 2021 and dated for five months before calling it quits.

DREAMS OF A FAMILY

Chrishell opened up to fans about their break-up in a vulnerable Instagram post, revealing that ideas about having children may have been the demise of their romance.

She wrote: "I have tried to embrace the world I have found myself in and that includes being open and honest about my relationships.

"It felt scary and terrifying knowing that it may not end in what society deems a success and what I am ultimately looking for. But after many long heartfelt talks, I am choosing to see it as a success no matter what label is attached to it publicly."

Chrishell continued: "Jason was and is my best friend, and other than our ideas for family ultimately not being aligned, the amount of respect and love we have for each other will not change going forward.

Further providing context, she explained: "Men have the luxury of time that women don't and that's just the way it goes. All of that being said, navigating this public terrain is difficult and I am just trying my best."

The reality star went on to say: "No one would rather only speak about work related things more than me. But I understand this comes with it and I will always love and be extremely grateful for the opportunities I have been given.

"Although my initial reaction to a private situation was to not speak on it, sometimes it's easier to just live transparently because we only get one chance at this life. I very much hope to one day have a family and decisions I make at this point are with that goal in mind."

Chrishell's message concluded: "Thank you for the kindness and support to those that understand."

THE BREAK-UP

Sources close to Chrishell and Jason confirmed their split to PEOPLE in December.

The pair appeared in an Instagram video posted by Chrishell a week prior.

No reason for the split was given at the time.

In an interview with E! News, Chrishell opened up about her relationship with Jason.

She said starting a romance was an "easy transition" for her and Jason because they "were working so much together as it was, and we work so well together."

Before the split, Jason gushed about Chrishell in an interview with The Sun.

He said at the time: "Chrishell and I became close friends and it has developed into an amazing relationship.
