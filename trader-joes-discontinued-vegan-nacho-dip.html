Trader Joe's confirms it has discontinued fan favorite dip and shoppers say it was 'the best' ever

Publish date: 2024-02-19

TRADER Joe’s has confirmed that a beloved dip product has been discontinued.

Fans loved the vegan alternative to a game night classic.

Trader Joe’s is loved by customers for its quirky and unusual products.

In recent years, the chain has been releasing more and more vegan alternatives to American classics.

One recent hit was the organic vegan nacho dip.

Sold in 8-ounce tubs, the item was only $3.99.

Read more food news

The dairy-free dip mimicked the texture of melted cheese with cashew nuts.

It could even be warmed in the microwave for a hot nacho sauce.

The dip has now been discontinued, a store associate confirmed to The U.S. Sun.

Shoppers looking for an alternative might enjoy the vegan Buffalo dip, organic hummus, or vegan tzatziki.

But the vegan nacho dip was so popular that many customers have even posted on Twitter to complain about it being discontinued.

“They discontinued the vegan nacho cheese dip at Trader Joe’s, I am unwell,” wrote one.

“I’ve been waiting for forever for Trader Joe’s to bring back the vegan nacho cheese dip,” posted another.

A third raged: “Trader Joe’s better have a legitimate reason for getting rid of the vegan nacho cheese.”

“Trader Joe’s vegan nacho dip is the best out there,” mourned another.

MORE DISCONTINUED ITEMS

This nacho dip is not the only popular product Trader Joe’s has recently discontinued. 

The novelty item chocolate hummus was scrapped last year.

And fans say the barbecue rub with coffee and garlic went way too fast - although the seasonal product will return in 2024.

But there is some good news for Trader Joe’s regulars, especially those who hate self-checkout.

The store will never add the controversial technology, chief executive officer Bryan Palbaum confirmed earlier this month.
