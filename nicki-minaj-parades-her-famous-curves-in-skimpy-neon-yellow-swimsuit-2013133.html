Publish date: 2024-07-21

Dangerous curves ahead! Nicki Minaj tweeted a slew of sexy pictures from her forthcoming “High School (feat. Lil Wayne)” music video on Tuesday, March 12. The 30-year-old rapper showed off her crazy cleavage and juicy booty in a neon yellow, bandage-style swimsuit.

The outspoken American Idol judge has no problem showing off her assets for the sake of her art. “I don’t mind the fascination . . .People are sitting in the barbershop talking about my butt. Minaj denied persistent plastic surgery rumors in a February 2013 interview with Extra. “I’ve never had surgery on my face. They’ll see contour and they’ll think you had surgery on your nose,” she explained. “No, no, no. Look at RuPaul’s Drag Race and you’ll see how you can make your nose look any shape you want.”
