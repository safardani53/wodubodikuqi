Pregnant Nikki Bella reveals ex-fiance John Cena made her cut wild sex stories from her memoir

Publish date: 2024-02-17

NIKKI Bella revealed her ex-fiance John Cena made her cut out the "wild sex stories" from her memoir, Incomparable.

The pregnant WWE vet spoke candidly about her upcoming book and past relationships on the daytime show, Better Together with Maria Menounos.

Nikki told Maria John, who has editing rights over the book, wanted one sex story involving him removed.

She admitted: "I understood. It was great for him!

"I was kinda like, don't you want high fives on that!?...

"We were wild, and he was strong...so it was a great story."

Nikki added: "With John though, there wasn't even anything bad written...It was more beautiful things that were taken out."

Nikki also said she and John ultimately ended what she described as an "incredible relationship" over disagreements about wanting children.

She said: "It was like, even in the end when he was willing to give me kids, I could just tell, it's not what we wanted.

"And that's really, like, you know, what pushed me in the end, was like, if I'm going to force someone to be a father...what if he looks at you down the road and just regrets everything, and then you have this child and you've built this life.

"Is that what you want? And I remember thinking, 'it's not what I want.'"

Nikki and her ex-fiance John were engaged before they broke up in 2018 after six years together.

She is now engaged and expecting her first child with her Dancing With the Stars partner Artem Chigvinstev.

She posted: "Excited for 2020 and the next decade with you @theartemc.

"I said yes in France in November!

"We have been trying to keep it a secret but really wanted to share our excitement for the New Year!"

Nikki and Brie Bella are both pregnant and due less than two weeks apart.

Nikki told People earlier this year: “Even though I feel really sick, like, can’t believe I’m going to be a mom soon.

"I’m going to have this person to take care of for the rest of their life and help them do the right things and just show them the way of the world. That’s amazing!”

In the interview, Brie and Nikki also reflected on the death of WWE star Shad Gaspard.

The 39-year-old ex WWE star was found dead and washed up on a beach yesterday in Los Angeles.

Brie said : "Oh my gosh. Shad was one of the sweetest human beings.

"Shad was one of those guys who was always so sweet and respectful to women, always made the Divas feel good.

"He always brought this light into a room."

Nikki added: "You never saw him not smile. You never saw him angry. He literally always lit up the room..."

Brie explained: "We all quarantined for 14 days. Could have NO contact with the outside world.

"And then once those tests came in, then, we were all are together, but still quarantined, like all isolated."

She went on: "It's actually nice because we get to get to talk to other humans..."
