Ainsley Earhardt Pics Of The Fox News Journalist &amp; Anchor Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-04-28

The pride of the Carolinas has taken the Big Apple by storm. Ainsley Earhardt – born in South Carolina, raised in North Carolina – has been part of Fox News since 2007. A graduate from University of South Carolina, the ‘Fox & Friends’ co-host first started out as part of Sean Hannity’s program, hosting the ‘Ainsley Across America’ segment. She had roles on ‘Fox & Friends Weekend,’ ‘Fox’s All-American New Year’s Eve,’ ‘America’s News Headquarters,’ and made appearances on ‘Red Eye’ and ‘The Live Desk.’ She became a ‘Fox & Friends’ co-host in 2016. A self-described ‘traditionalist,’ Ainsley has allowed her deep-rooted Christian faith shape her politics. She is known to be one of Donald Trump’s dedicated supporters, but her friends and colleagues credit her for being at least willing to listen to the other side of a debate. In 2018, she and her husband Will Proctor separated. They divorced the following year, agreeing to co-parent their daughter.
