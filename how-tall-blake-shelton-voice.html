How tall is Blake Shelton?

Publish date: 2024-02-06

COUNTRY music star Blake Shelton was a beloved coach on NBC's The Voice for 12 years before his departure came following season 23.

Fans of the 47-year-old TV personality want to know more about his personal life and marriage to fellow music icon, Gwen Stefani.

How tall is Blake Shelton?

Blake Shelton is 6-foot-4 inches tall.

"It’s easier for them to find clothes," Shelton joked to EW in 2014, referring to shorter television stars.

"That’s the sucky thing about being tall and being a fat-a** at the same time. Nothing fits.

"If you see me on red carpets and I’ve got a look of disgust on my face, it’s because I’m uncomfortable with whatever I have to be wearing."

He added: "They called me Hook-shot Shelton back in Oklahoma, if that tells you anything.

"You can tell by looking at me that I am an athletic son of a b***h. I don’t want to brag, but I still wear my Reebok Pumps from high school."

Shelton's former co-star on The Voice, Kelly Clarkson, told Late Night with Seth Meyers: “I do go in on Blake a lot though. He’s kind of a giant target."

She would add: "Every time we do a photo together for, like, any promo you see, I’m always on two steps, or otherwise I look like an Oompa Loompa because I’m 5-3."

What is Blake Shelton's net worth?

Shelton is believed to have a net worth of $120million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

The All Over Me singer reportedly made around $13million per cycle on The Voice since joining the show in 2011.

Shelton - who has 28 number-one country singles to his name - has received nine Grammy Award nominations throughout his music career.

He has over 8.9million monthly listeners on Spotify and more than 6million followers on Instagram.

Is Blake Shelton married?

Blake Shelton married Gwen Stefani in an intimate ceremony at his Oklahoma ranch on July 3, 2021.

The pair initially started dating in 2015 after meeting on the set of The Voice.

Shelton ended up proposing on October 27, 2020, and announced their engagement on social media writing: "Hey Gwen, thanks for saving my 2020... And the rest of my life.. I love you. I heard a YES!"

In July 2023, rumors began to swirl that tension may be growing between Stefani and Shelton. According to In Touch, a source told Star: “Times are tough for Gwen and Blake.

“They’re spending a lot of time apart and the relationship is suffering. Friends are worried that if they don’t address their problems before they spiral out of control, divorce might be in the cards for them."
