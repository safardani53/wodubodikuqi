When will the music festival take place?

Ultra Music Festival is a three-day fest taking place from Friday, March 22, through Sunday, March 24, 2024.

The event caps off Miami Music Week, a weeklong event of electronic dance music.

The festival is located at Bayfront Park in downtown Miami.

How can I buy tickets?

Tickets to #Ultra2024 are available now.
