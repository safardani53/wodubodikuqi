I found Birkenstock dupes at Walmart for at least 67% cheaper and theyre more comfortable

Publish date: 2024-04-14

A TIKTOK user has flaunted a discount pair of sandals she picked up from Walmart that were nearly identical to Birkenstock footwear.

Birkenstock has established a reputation as a fashionable brand, and its prices reflect the high demand for its shoes.

The company's cheapest strapped sandals for adults go for $49.95, while most variations retail for closer to $100.

But as TikToker laineysloopsboutique highlighted in a recent video, much of that price can be chalked up to the name on the shoe.

Linda, who runs the account, said she found sandals at Walmart that was nearly identical to a pair of Birkenstock shoes she recently got.

She spent just $15 per pair, meaning she could get at least three pairs for the price of one Birkenstock set.

Not only that, she said the Walmart dupe - from footwear brand Time and Tru - was more comfortable than her Birkenstocks.

Walmart sells Time and Tru women's sandals for around $15 to $20 depending on store location.

Linda showed off a second pair of Time and Tru sandals that had suffered some water damage and said even that pair was more comfortable than her Birkenstocks.

In the comments of her video, other women noted how uncomfortable Birkenstocks can be and some added that they love Time and Tru sandals.

"I got some from Walmart for $5 and they’ve lasted so long," one commenter said.

Unlike Birkenstock, however, Time and Tru doesn't produce any footwear for men.

More discounted duplicates at Walmart

One way Walmart makes good on its slogan of helping shoppers "save more" and "live better" is by selling items that are similar to high-end products but at a lower price, like the shoes Linda found.

A different Walmart shopper took to TikTok after finding deep discounts on the store's shoes, including $3 for Cushionaire and Birkenstock alternatives.

Another TikTok user reported saving more than 50% on a powerful insect trap by shopping at Walmart.

The store also sells a number of over-the-counter medications and personal care products that are cheaper than name-brand goods but work as well.

Multiple fitness fans have found Lululemon dupes at Walmart, saving close to $100 in some cases.

And cosmetic expert Rocio Soria has shared a list of her favorite alternatives to high-end makeups that sell for under $5.
