Watch UFC London star Tom Aspinall complete dramatic body transformation as he weighs in at career H

Publish date: 2024-04-01

TOM ASPINALL tipped the scales at his heaviest-ever weight ahead of his octagon return.

The Atherton assassin returns to action on Saturday night in the main event of UFC London.

UFC LONDON LIVE - TOM ASPINALL VS MARCIN TYBURA

Aspinall will be welcomed back to the cage by Polish pounder Marcin Tybura. 

The Team Kaobon star’s clash with Tybura tops the bill of a stacked card at The O2 Arena - nearly a year after he suffered a horrific knee injury. 

The small matter of making weight was on the agenda for the duo early on Friday morning.

And both men had no issue making the 266lb non-title fight heavyweight limit. 

Surging contender Aspinall beat Tybura to the scales and looked cool, calm and collected before weighing in.

Aspinall trimmed some of the timber he used to have but had clearly put on some mass during his time on the sidelines - coming in at solid and a career-heavy 258lbs (18.4stone).

Tybura, who will be looking to spoil the party, came in at 246lbs (17.5stone).
