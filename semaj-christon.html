Career

Semaj Christon started his basketball career at Winton Woods High School. After graduating from high school, he joined the Xavier University Musketeers for the 2012-13 season. On November 13, 2012, he made his Musketeer debut against Butler. Following his rookie season in 2012-2013, he was named Rookie of the Year and All-Atlantic 10 second-team accolades after scoring the second-most points (456) in Xavier history.

In 2014, he started his professional basketball career. On June 26, 2014, Christon was picked by the Miami Heat with the 55th overall selection in the 2014 NBA Draft. He was traded to the Charlotte Hornets shortly after the draft. Soon after, he was dealt to the Oklahoma City Thunder.

Christon played in the 2014 NBA Summer League for the Thunder, where he got his first taste of professional basketball. Later following summer, on August 3, 2015, he signed with Consultinvest Pesaro in the Italian Serie A. Christon rejoined the Oklahoma City Thunder for the 2016 NBA Summer League in July 2016. On August 20, 2016, he signed his first contract with the Thunder. On October 26, 2016, he made his NBA debut in a 103-97 victory against the Philadelphia 76ers.
