Who is Pink's husband, Carey Hart?

Publish date: 2024-03-12

IT IS fair to say Pink and Carey Hart have had their ups and downs over the years.

Despite breaking up at one point, the singer and her husband said they are stronger than ever thanks to couple's counselling.

Who is Pink's husband, Carey Hart?

Born in Seal Beach, California on July 17, 1975, 47-year-old Carey started his career as an American freestyle motocross racer and off-road racer.

In 2004, along with John Huntington, he opened Hart & Huntington Tattoo Company in Las Vegas, Nevada.

They later launched their own clothing line based on the shop.

A&E's reality television show, Inked, followed Carey and the workings of his shop.

Carey has appeared in a number of Pink's music videos.

How long has the couple been married and do they have children?

Carey and Pink met at the 2001 X Games in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania and dated for four years.

Pink proposed to him in June 2005 via a pit board during one of his races in Mammoth Lakes, California.

She held up a sign that asked "Will you marry me?".

He ignored her at first and completed another lap of the race.

Pink then changed the sign to read "I'm serious!" and Carey then pulled out of the race to pick Pink up after that.

They married at the Four Seasons resort in Costa Rica on January 7, 2006.

The couple split in 2008 but got back together the following year.

The couple has two children, including a son and a daughter.

Their daughter Willow Sage was born on June 2, 2011.

They then had a second child, Jameson Moon, who arrived on December 26, 2016.
