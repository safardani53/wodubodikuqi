Who is John Lugo? Real Housewives of Cheshire star Rachel's husband and property developer

Publish date: 2024-04-21

THE Real Housewives of Cheshire series nine is on ITVBe, but what about the husbands behind the women?

Who is Rachel Lugo's other half and property landlord John? We've got the lowdown on him..

Who is John Lugo?

John is a property entrepreneur married to Rachel Lugo, who joined the cast of The Real Housewives of Cheshire for series six.

He lives with his wife in a Grade II listed property in the exclusive area of Alderley Edge.

They are both landlords and have five children Francesca, 19, William, 18, Sophia, 15, Oliver, 12 and Sienna, six.

In the first episode of the new series, John revealed how much he wanted his wife to have their sixth baby, as he planned her surprise birthday party.

Rachel responded, saying: "Five years of sleepless nights and this body getting ruined again? No.

"I'm not having another baby. Five children we've got, he can't be serious."

How long have John and Rachel Lugo been together?

John and Rachel met in a restaurant 23 years ago and have been together ever since.

They couple married 18 years ago, on March 17.
