Marla Maples Bio Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-10-08

Bio:
Marla Maples (born Marla Maples in Cohutta, Georgia on October 27, 1963) is a model who is currently competing on season 22 of Dancing with the Stars, with partner, Tony Dovolani. She became famous in 1993 when she married Donald Trump — but they separated only four years later. She has also starred in many movies including “Maximum Overdrive,” “Executive Decisions” and “Black and White.”

Best Known For:
Marla Maples is best known for being married to Donald Trump; together they have daughter, Tiffany Ariana Trump, who is 22.

Personal Life:
Marla Maples grew up in Georgia and was a homecoming queen! However, it was her relationship later that made her famous; Donald Trump’s marriage to Ivana Zelnickova came to a halt when the media began claiming he had an affair with Marla, and they split in 1991. Marla had a daughter with Donald on October 13, 1993 — and the two married two months alter. However, they separated in May 1997 and divorced in 1999.
