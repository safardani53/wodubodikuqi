Publish date: 2024-04-17

THERE are a few unspoken rules when it comes to being a wedding guest, but the most importnat one is not to wear white.

One woman might've missed that memo when she showed up to her friend's big day in an 'edgy' outfit though.

Not only was her dress of choice white, but it was also a bit much for some people's taste.

It featured a white bodysuit with a sheer fabric over the top and a thigh-high slit.

A snap of the anonymous guest was shared on the Facebook group That's it, I'm wedding shaming where she was utterly rinsed for her risque outfit.

The post read: "That is not that bride. And yes, that seems to be a body suit under the gauze fabric.
