See the Wheel of Time cast in and out of costume, including Rosamund Pike, Daniel Henney, and more

Publish date: 2024-10-03

THE Wheel of Time stars Rosamund Pike, Daniel Henney, and more cast members look completely different in and out of their costumes. 

Season 1 of Amazon’s new fantasy series, which is based on Robert Jordan’s magical novels of the same name, premiered on Friday with its first three episodes.

According to the synopsis, the show “follows Moiraine, a member of the incredibly powerful all-female organization called the Aes Sedai, as she arrives in the small town of Two Rivers. 

“There, she embarks on a dangerous, world-spanning journey with five young men and women, one of whom is prophesied to be the Dragon Reborn, who will either save or destroy humanity.”

Keep reading to see what the Wheel of Time cast looks likes in real life compared to their onscreen characters, who are all dressed in high fantasy costumes.

Rosamund Pike (Moiraine Damodred)

The 42-year-old actress leads the fantasy series as a member of Aes Sedai, an organization of women with magical powers.

For the role, she swapped out her usual dirty-blonde locks for long, brunette hair.

Previously, Rosamund made a name for herself in movies like I Care A Lot, Gone Girl, and Jack Reacher, and Die Another Day.

Daniel Henney (Lan Mondragoran)

To portray the show’s exceptional swordsman, the Criminal Minds alum wore his hair slicked back and kept in place with a band around his forehead.

Daniel traded his suits and casual clothing for wrist armor, layers of cloth, and a sword sheathed across his back.

The 41-year-old recently wrapped his run as Matt Simmon in the Criminal Minds universe.

He’s also known for his role as Agent Zero in X-Men origins: Wolverine and his voice work in Big Hero 6.

Josha Stradowski (Rand al’Thor)

While he’s got a head full of orange-brown hair on The Wheel of time, Josha recently shaved it all off for a buzzcut look.

The 26-year-old Dutch actor plays a sheepherder on the series and wears clothing that works for his farm boy character, including casual clothing and a fur jacket.

Madeleine Madden (Egwene al’Vere)

The 24-year-old Australian actress keeps a dagger close to her on the show and stays warm in a long, brown, coat with an intricate belt at its middle.

Never knowing what trouble she’ll come up on, her character keeps her hair out of the way, all pulled back, with just a few pieces straggling out.

Recently, Madeleine starred in the 2019 movie Dora and the Lost City of Gold, and before that, she was on shows like Ready for This, Picnic at Hanging Rock, Pine Gap, and Tidelands.

Marcus Rutherford (Perrin Aybara)

While in real life Marcus styles his hair with a closer shave, his Wheel of Time character has it grown out a bit longer.

He wears a fur-sleeved, sturdy jacket on the show, which is kept together with a link chain.

Marcus could previously be seen on the crime series Bulletproof and movies County Lines and Obey.

Zoë Robins (Nynaeve al’Meara)

In real life, Zoë showed off her shoulder-length hair at the Amazon show’s premiere red carpet.

On the series, her character has long hair, past her waist, that’s often styled in a braid.

The actress is best known for playing the White Ninja Steel Ranger on Power Rangers Ninja Steel from 2017 to 2018.

She also starred opposite Imogen Poots in the 2019 horror-thriller, Black Christmas.

Barney Harris (Mat Cauthon)

For the show, Barney let all of his facial hair grow in for more of a scraggly appearance and his character completes the look with ill-fitting, more threadbare clothing.

Previously, he worked with Sarah Jessica Parker on the movie All Roads Lead to Rome and with Joe Alwyn on Billy Lynn's Long Halftime Walk.
