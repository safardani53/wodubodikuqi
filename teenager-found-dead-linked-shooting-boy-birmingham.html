Teenager, 17, is found dead near canal with cops saying its linked to murder of boy, 16, in

Publish date: 2024-05-15

POLICE investigating the fatal shooting of a 16-year-old boy have found the body of a second teenager.

The grim discovery was made this morning as officers carried out searches as part of the original killing.

Police confirmed the boy, believed to be aged around 17, was found next to a canal in Birmingham.

His death has been linked to the shooting of the 16-year-old, which took place at a home in Ladywood on Sunday afternoon.

He was rushed to hospital but sadly passed away two days later from his injuries.

Officers confirmed they are not looking for anyone else in connection with today's discovery.

A 17-year-old boy arrested as part of the initial murder probe has been released on bail while enquiries continue.

Det Supt Shaun Edwards, from our Homicide team, said: "This is a tragic development in our investigation, and our thoughts are with everyone who has been impacted by the events of the last week.

"Our investigation into what has happened will continue for some time, and we will be supporting the families involved throughout.

"Neighbourhood officers continue to patrol the area to offer reassurance, and I'd encourage anyone with information to get in touch with us."
