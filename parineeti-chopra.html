Parineeti Chopra Biography

Publish date: 2024-05-28

Parineeti Chopra is currently at the age of 28. Chopra belongs to South Asian ethnicity and holds Indian nationality. She always wanted to become an investment banker but later She started off her career as a PR which made her gain knowledge about the field of movies and that become her passion.

Chopra's age was just 23 when she got her first break in Bollywood film, she made her first debut as a supporting actress in the movie Ladies VS Ricky Bahl and her performance as a supporting actress was very much appreciated in the movie, she was even nominated for a Film Fare award for best supporting actress and also managed to win best female debut for her performance.

Following this movie, She appeared in romantical political drama film Ishaqzaade with Arjun Kapoor.
