Kaia Gerber follows in her mum Cindy Crawford's footsteps &amp; stuns as she models for Calvin Klein

Publish date: 2024-05-01

MUM’S the word but model Kaia Gerber is certainly fashioning a name for herself in the family business.

The daughter of 1990s catwalk star Cindy Crawford, 55, shows off her tattoos in white underwear for a new Calvin Klein campaign.

Kaia, 19, walked in her first Calvin Klein fashion show back in 2018, copying her model mum, 55, who was a regular fixture on the designer’s runways.

The star has also been branching out into acting after landing a role in super producer Ryan Murphy’s new edition of American Horror Story.
