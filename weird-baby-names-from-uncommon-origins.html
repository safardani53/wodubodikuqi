I'd like to call my future daughter Salmonella - it's cute, she could go by Ella, what's not to love

Publish date: 2024-04-27

THIS TikToker has shared weird baby names like Salmonella that she thought would be cute if no one knew their original meaning.

The influencer who goes by @gailolee on TikTok gave a list of baby names that she thinks are a hit, but others might find bizarre.

She started off the video with the name Chlamydia.

"You can't tell me that isn't a pretty name if it didn't mean STD," she said.

Someone agreed with her saying "Okay but chlamydia is a great name" in the comment section.

Next up is a very scientific name - Analysis.

"I think that's such a cute little girl's name like so pretty," she said.

The TikToker jokingly said the name Omelette "sounds like an egg-cellent name to me."

Dyslexia was another one of her top names saying that you could shorten it to Lexi and called it "brilliant."

The name Duvet - "it's fun, it's fresh, it's fancy," the TikToker said.

She believes the name "gives the impression of a very well reserved person."

Next up is Sanity, which the influencer jokingly said "aside from my lack of, that would make a brilliant name."

Lastly, she offered up the name Salmonella.

While you might think of a bad stomach flu for this one, the TikToker sees potential in the name.

"You could even shorten it to Ella," she said.

"It's cute, it's got a nickname, what's not to love?"

People found this video very amusing with commenters praising the creator's name suggestions and coming up with their own as well.

"Omg I love it, especially analysis," one person said.

Another commented: "I love this, ages ago I was told there was someone called blanket so not far of duvet."

"I’ve always said alopecia," a third suggested.
