The Body Shop launches CBD Collection for stressed out skin - here's whats included

Publish date: 2024-02-15

THE Body Shop has launched a CBD Collection for stressed out skin, and we cant wait to get our hands on it.

The three step collection has been sourced exclusively from hemp plants and includes wonder product CBD.

The range includes three products, including a Mask, Oil, and Cream.

  • CBD Collection, from £17, The Body Shop - buy here

The CBD Collection looks to soothe stressed skin, and is sourced exclusively from hemp plants; although The Body Shop have been championing hemp long before it was cool.

The range includes a CBD Soothing Oil-Balm Cleansing Mask (£17), CBD Restoring Facial Oil (£25) and CBD Replenishing Moisture Cream (£20).

All three of the products have been designed to tackle the effects of stressed skin; which they categorise as dry, dehydrated and lacking in luminosity, with a magical mixture of CBD, hemp seed oil, squalene and patchouli extract.

What's included in the CBD Collection?

CBD Soothing Oil-Balm Cleansing Mask 100ml, £17

Hit refresh with a mask that does more, so you can rest your way to skin that feels reset. This innovative balm-to-oil-to-milk texture is enriched with hydrating olive oil, purifying white clay and a blend of CBD and hemp seed oil, to help condition the skin while shifting away all dirt and grime.
Performing like a daily wash-off treatment, this vegan formula should
be applied to dry skin. Leave to work for 3-5 minutes then rinse to reveal
smoother, squeaky-clean skin.

CBD Restoring Facial Oil 30ml, £25

Dial back to the basics with this blend of natural essential oils, infused with
patchouli extract, CBD and hemp seed oils. Base your routine around this
delicate, dry facial oil to help soothe signs of dullness, replenish moisture levels and enhance the experience of other products in the collection.
Shake a few drops into the palm of the hand and dab in small dots onto
cleansed skin before smoothing over the face, morning and night.

CBD Replenishing Moisture Cream 50ml, £20

A friend to all skin types but especially dehydrated skin, this rich and velvety vegan cream combines CBD and hemp seed oil with squalane and patchouli extract to help relieve and calm the signs of dryness.
Warm the formula between the fingers for a few seconds until it becomes
translucent, then press gently into the skin. For added nourishment, usethis following the CBD Restoring Facial Oil whenever you feel like you need it.
