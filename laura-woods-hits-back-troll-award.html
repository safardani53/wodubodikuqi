Laura Woods brutally hits back at troll who called her the 'worst presenter in sport' as fans hail '

Publish date: 2024-03-16

LAURA WOODS offered the perfect response when criticised by a troll on social media.

Successful presenter Laura is one of the nation's most popular TV stars.

Last night she hosted action from Tottenham Hotspur Stadium as Spurs beat Burnley 1-0 in the FA Cup.

During the game, one grump fumed: "Laura woods have to be the worst presenter in sport."

Laura, 36, was quick to offer the perfect response.

Replying to the troll, named Darren, she posted a picture of her Sports Presenter of the Year award.
