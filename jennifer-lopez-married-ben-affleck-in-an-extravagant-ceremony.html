Jennifer Lopez Married Ben Affleck In An Extravagant Ceremony

Publish date: 2024-08-05

Jennifer Lopez married Ben Affleck in a grandiose ceremony in Georgia on August 20, 2022, after their July court-wedding in Las Vegas. Jennifer and Ben recruited Jay Shetty, a life coach and one of the world’s most prominent motivational speakers, to officiate the wedding. Shetty was born in the United Kingdom but is of Indian origin, and he is well-known as a presenter, storyteller, filmmaker, and content developer. Aside from that, he is a former monk.

Jennifer Lopez has been friends with Jay Shetty for how long?

Shetty and Jennifer Lopez had previously appeared in a couple of episodes of the YouTube series Coach Conversations. Aside from that, she featured on Shetty’s podcast, On Purpose, in March 2021. Shetty said in a Facebook post in February 2022 that Lopez had not only asked him to officiate four marriages but also to talk about the power of love at her special Marry Me performance. Jay Shetty has officiated at a number of weddings, including those of Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck. He also officiated at Lilly Collins and Charlie McDowell’s wedding in Colorado last year.

Shetty spent three years as a monk in a monastery in Mumbai, India, after graduating from a UK business school. When he returned to the corporate sector, he was engaged to create films for the Huffington Post in New York. Shetty conducted interviews with celebrities such as Kobe Bryant and Russell Simmons for his YouTube videos on mental health, wellbeing, and relationships. Shetty married Radhi, his long-term lover, in 2016. They co-own Sama Tea, an Ayurvedic-inspired tea company. Shetty was also named one of Forbes’ 30 under 30 in Europe in 2017. Aside from that, he has been featured on the BBC and has been asked to talk at several corporations such as Google, Barclays, and others.

In 2019, he began his own podcast, On Purpose, which had more than 64 million downloads in its first year. Priyanka Chopra, Novak Djokovic, Matt Damon, and other celebrities have been on the program. In 2020, Shetty published his book Think Like a Monk, which debuted on the New York Times and Amazon bestseller lists. In 2022, he became the first Chief Purpose Officer of Calm, a meditation-production firm.

Bennifer’s Georgia wedding details

Following their Las Vegas wedding, Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck married again in a three-day “intimate” ceremony in Georgia attended by the couple’s closest friends and family. The wedding weekend began on Friday, August 19, with a rehearsal supper. The ceremony took place on August 20, followed by a picnic on August 21. The celebration was held at Affleck’s Riceboro estate.

Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck got engaged for the second time this year in April. They’ve known one other since 2002 when they began dating. They also got engaged shortly at the time but split up a few months later.
