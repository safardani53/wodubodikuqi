The Beckhams show off glam Christmas - but fans are distracted after spotting surprising detail

Publish date: 2024-09-23

THE Beckhams live a life of almost unimaginable luxury with a string of homes and millions in the bank.

However, fans spotted one item in the famous family's Christmas celebrations that made them think they're just like us after all.

Hidden in a video showing the highlights of their festive lunch, followers noticed they had the £5 Pyrex jug that will been used for the gravy in countless British homes.

One fan on TikTok wrote: "It's nice to see that even the Beckhams use a Pyrex jug for the gravy."

Another agreed: "I thought exactly the same." A third chipped in: "I came here to say the same!!"

The video showed the 'real' side of the Beckhams, who are thought to be worth £450million from David and Victoria's combined wealth alone.

The former England captain was shown vacuuming underneath the Christmas tree while the ex-Spice Girl, 49, was seen complaining about emptying the rubbish.

She said, tying up the Christmas Day leftovers: "I bet that Jennifer Lopez doesn't do bins."

David, 48, who was filming her, replied in the deadpan fashion viewers will know from his recent Netflix documentary: "But you're not Jennifer Lopez to be fair."

The fashion designer looked much more glam earlier in the video when she sashayed into the room in a dressing gown and Santa hat.

The video also showed David and his eldest son Brooklyn, 24 - who is a budding chef - taking care of the dinner and carefully assembling a pile of roast potatoes.

His wife Nicola Peltz, 28, also featured, as did the Beckhams' only daughter Harper, 12, who was shown receiving a dollop of gravy from the Pyrex container.

Victoria was seen cosying up to her daughter-in-law too, with the pair blowing kisses in a show of unity that suggests they've put their alleged falling out behind them.

Another image earlier from Victoria's Instagram account showed David about to carve the turkey and roast beef as he enjoyed a glass of wine.

The picture appeared to show them in their estate in the Cotswolds - while the other images appeared to be in their home in Miami, suggesting the family enjoyed TWO Christmas days.

Victoria posted: "We love you so much @davidbeckham X Happy Christmas, you are our everything, we love you so much xxxx @brooklynpeltzbeckham @romeobeckham @cruzbeckham #harperseven."

One Beckhams fan wrote on the video, which was shared by Nicola: "Love seeing these snippets of the family. You guys are so cute."
