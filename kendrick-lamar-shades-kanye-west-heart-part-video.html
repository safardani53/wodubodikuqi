KENDRICK Lamar shaded Kanye West by morphing into the rapper in the music video for his new song, The Heart Part 5.

The Pulitzer Prize winner, 34, has taken several swipes at the Yeezy mogul, 44, over the years.

And Kendrick appeared to take aim at Kanye yet again in the latest song from his iconic "The Heart" series.

In the song, which samples Marvin Gaye's hit I Want You, he uses deepfake technology to transform his face into Kanye's.

The rapper appears at the start of the clip as himself, wearing a white T-shirt and bandana.

His clothes stay the same as his face morphs into Kanye's as he raps a verse.

Kendrick also morphs into OJ Simpson, Will Smith, Jussie Smollett, Kobe Bryant, and Nipsey Hussle in the video.

The track is from his new album Mr Morale & The Big Steppers - his final album with Top Dawg Entertainment - which is dropping on Friday.

Kendrick and Kanye have publicly feuded in the past.

Back in 2015, Kendrick took a shot at Kanye in his song King Kunta from his album To Pimp A Butterfly.

He raps: "The line goes like this: "I can dig rapping, but a rapper with a ghostwriter?/ What the f**k happened?/

"(Oh no) I swore I wouldn't tell/ But most of y'all sharing bars like you got the bottom bunk in a two-man cell/ Something's in the water/

"And if I got a brown nose for some gold then I'd rather be a bum than a motherf**kin' baller."

Kanye has long been rumored to employ ghostwriters to help out with his songs.

BAD BLOOD

Last year, Kendrick appeared to take another swipe in Baby Keem's song Family Ties.

He raps: "Yeah, Kanye changed his life /But me, I'm still an old school Gemini (Lil' b***h)"

Kendrick and Kanye are both Geminis but in 2002 Kanye declared that he was born again Christian and said he was only going to make secular music.

Kendrick appeared to be alluding that he hadn't changed whereas Kanye was now drastically different.

YE'S FEUDS

Kanye is no stranger to getting into verbal wars with various notable stars.

Earlier this year, he took aim at his ex-wife Kim Kardashian and her boyfriend Pete Davidson.

Kanye, 44, called Pete a "d**khead" and even insisted the funnyman will "never" meet any of the four kids he shares with his ex-wife.

The hitmaker, who has been referring to Pete as "Skete," even claimed the actor is the reason he hasn't been able to reunite with his baby mama.
