Kourtney Kardashian SLAMMED for Sharing Photo of Her Children?!

Publish date: 2024-06-14

Kourtney Kardashian has always been the mellow Kardashian, the laid back one, the least scandalous of all of her sisters.

Or, well, at least she used to be.

Things started changing when her longtime love, Scott Disick, began that intense downward spiral of his, and when she coped with the heartbreak from their split by hooking up with Justin Bieber, her reputation just got rockier.

But Kourtney just shared these new photos of her two youngest children, Penelope and Reign, sitting on top of a Mercedes G-Wagon.

And it’s safe to say that the pictures are becoming pretty scandalous.

One issue that people are having with the photo is a safety issue — we know Kourtney is pretty short, but still, those kids are awfully high off the ground and for no good reason.

But mostly Kourtney’s followers are seeing this as a tacky display of wealth, which would be bad enough, but it’s even worse because the family still seems to be reeling from Kim’s robbery.

And on top of that, Kendall Jenner was robbed earlier this month.

And on top of that, a new scene from Keeping Up with the Kardashians shows Kylie Jenner seriously freaking out over a possible break-in at her home.

As one of Kourtney’s helpful followers pointed out, photos like these are "why yo asses are getting robbed."

"Did you not learn a valuable lesson about displaying materialistic items????" one fan wondered. "So you basically just showed someone what kind of car you drive… I thought you were the smarter sister!"

Another follower urged Kourtney to "Teach your children the value of life! Being so materialistic is going against what God teaches…"

One person dealt her a low blow, writing "For f-ck’s sake, seriously? Materialistic much? Your dad is rolling in his grave."

And one suggested that "Maybe all these pics trying to show off are the reasons you all keep getting robbed. I would never take pics of my cars, jewelry, etc… looks desperate and not classy."

Ain’t that the truth?

There’s just no reason for this kind of thing, and you’d really think that after what happened to Kim, her family would realize the benefits of, you know, not showing off expensive things you own on social media.

And you’d also think that, since Kourtney is apparently so concerned about the possible cancelation of Keeping Up with the Kardashians, she’d be on top of her "Don’t Inspire Robbers to Come Find Me" game.

Just a few days ago, we heard that Kourtney was "livid" with Kim for the reality show’s failing ratings, because she "really has nothing else going on," so losing the show would be a disaster for her.

But sure, girl, keep sharing all your fancy items on Instagram. Hey, maybe even hand out free keys to your front door.

Whatever, right?
