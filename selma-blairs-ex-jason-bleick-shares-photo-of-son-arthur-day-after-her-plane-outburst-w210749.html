Selma Blairs Ex Jason Bleick Shares Photo of Son Arthur Day After Her Plane Outburst

Publish date: 2024-09-18

Selma Blair’s ex-boyfriend Jason Bleick shared a photo of their son, Arthur, on Instagram on Tuesday, June 21, just one day after Blair was reportedly hospitalized after having a mid-flight incident.

“A new day & an early morning stick hunt with dad. #arthursaintbleick #everydayisbeautiful,” Bleick captioned the photo of their adorable 4-year-old son playing at Los Angeles’ Bronson Canyon. 

The fashion designer also shared a selfie featuring Arthur on the plane on their way back from Cancun, Mexico, on Monday, June 20, but it’s unclear if they were on the same flight as Blair. The three had been vacationing together for Father’s Day.

On Sunday, Blair was spotted looking relaxed in a white bikini while hanging out with her ex and son at the Hard Rock Hotel Riviera Maya. “Selma seemed happy and healthy all weekend,” a source at the hotel told Us Weekly.
