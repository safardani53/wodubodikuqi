Blueface's girlfriend Chrisean Rock breaks silence after rapper's shock arrest and vows to get face

Publish date: 2024-05-29

BLUEFACE'S girlfriend Chrisean Rock has vowed to get a tattoo on her face to honor the rapper after he was arrested.

The 22-year-old musician, whose real name is Chrisean Malone, took to her Instagram Stories on Tuesday night to discuss her boyfriend's shocking arrest for attempted murder.

Blueface, whose real name is Johnathan Jamall Porter, was arrested outside Lo-Lo's Chicken & Waffles in Las Vegas in an undercover investigation on Tuesday.

The 25-year-old was pinned to the ground by six to eight officers who had parked in unmarked cars, sources told TMZ.

Chrisean was with Blueface when he was arrested.

He was arrested for felony attempted murder with the use of a deadly firearm or tear gas, according to legal documents obtained by TMZ.

The rapper's charges were in connection with a shooting that took place on October 8 in Paradise, Nevada, according to the police.

Hours after Blueface's arrest, Chrisean declared that she is standing by the rapper.

The influencer said on her Instagram Stories: "I'm going to court tomorrow and that's all that matters.

"He coming home, I don't know what y'all talking about."

Chrisean already has seven tattoos dedicated to Blueface, with whom she has been in an on-off relationship since 2020.

They are all either his portraits or scripts of his legal name, Johnathan Porter.

Blueface and Rock have a history of relationship issues from physical fights to cheating scandals.
