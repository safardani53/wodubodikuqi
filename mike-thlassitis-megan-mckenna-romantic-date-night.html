Muggy Mike Thalassitis confirms he's back with Megan McKenna as he shares video of their romantic da

Publish date: 2024-07-17

MIKE Thalassitis confirmed his reconciliation with girlfriend Megan McKenna as they shared a cosy date night last night.

Showing the world that the pair are very much back together, the Love Island star, 25, even posted moments from their romantic meal on his Instagram story.

The first clip showed Megan sitting opposite him at the table as they enjoyed in dinner at her McK Grill restaurant.

"Losing my @MCKGRILL virginity," he cheekily wrote across the video, which showed Megan, also 25, smiling coyly.

A second post showed off the delights the pair were indulging as former Towie star Megan looked lovingly at him.

The Sun Online revealed the pair had rekindled their romance, just weeks after they called time on their two-month relationship.

A source said: "They're talking again and they're back together. They wanted to make it work - after the split, they knew the relationship wasn't over, and started talking again.

“They really like each other and want to make things work. They're so on and off though - things are so volatile - that their mates are trying to stay out of it."

The couple were seen having a blazing row in the street last week after reconciling again.

An onlooker revealed that Megan and Mike had reunited to celebrate his brother’s birthday, however during the final stop in their bar crawl Mike was apparently separated from the group that led to a bust-up on the street, in which he accused her of flirting with other men.
