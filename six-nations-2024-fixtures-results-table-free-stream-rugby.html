Six Nations 2024 fixtures and results: England face Italy, Ireland play France &amp; Wales host Scotland

Publish date: 2024-09-23

THE 2024 Six Nations is less than one month away as the northern hemisphere's best meet once again.

Ireland were victorious at last year's tournament and claimed a historic Grand Slam in the process.

Andy Farrell will be without fly-half and captain Jonny Sexton after he retired at the end of the Rugby World Cup.

England will be aiming to improve on last year's Six Nations finish after they came 4th in the standings and claimed just two wins at the tournament.

Red Rose will be without captain Owen Farrell after he made himself unavailable in order to "prioritise his and his family’s mental wellbeing".

On opening weekend, champions Ireland will travel to 2023 Six Nations runners-up France to kick the tournament off.

England will be hoping to get off to a winning start as they face last year's bottom side Italy, while Wales host Scotland.

When is Six Nations 2024?

  • Six Nations 2024 will get underway on Friday, February 2, 2024 before concluding on Saturday, March 16, 2024.
  • This is the 130th edition of the huge competition.

How can I watch the Six Nations?

  • Fixtures this year are set to be shown LIVE on both BBC and ITV in the UK.
  • Live streams will be available on the FREE BBC iPlayer app and FREE ITVX app (providing you have a valid TV license) available to download on your mobile or tablet devices.
  • Alternatively, SunSport will be live blogging every match at the 2024 Six Nations so you can keep up to date with all the action.

Can I watch it for FREE?

  • You can watch the 2024 Six Nations for FREE via the BBC iPlayer app and the ITVX app, providing you have a valid TV license.
  • You can download both the BBC iPlayer app and the ITVX app for free onto your mobile or tablet device.
