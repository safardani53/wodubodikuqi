Halloween Costumes: Your Star-Inspired Getups!

Publish date: 2024-04-27

JACK SPARROW

Erik Rehfeld of Monroe, Wash., writes: "I am a perfectionist when it comes to costumes so I spent almost a year piecing this costume together…I had a lot of help from my mom and wife with input and won 1st place in the Halloween costume contest at a local bar."

KATY PERRY & SNOOP DOGG

Dana Powell of Pittsburgh, Pa., writes: "My husband and I decided to go as Katy Perry and Snoop Dog from the "California Gurls" video. I tracked down the same fabric used for Snoop's costume and with the help of a friend, put together a three-piece suit. I also tried to recreate Katy's costume as best I could. We just loved the fun, loud style of these costumes."

KHLOE, KOURTNEY & KIM KARDASHIAN

Erika Sherwood of San Diego, Calif., writes: "The Kardashian sisters complete with baby Mason."

REAL HOUSEWIVES OF ATLANTA

Cordelia Tullous of Houston, Texas, writes: "All of us attend law school together and are fans of the Real Housewives of Atlanta so we dressed as them for our Halloween party. We are from left to right: Lindsay Bealor (Kim), Tamecia Harris (Kandi), Tiffani Walker (Sheree), Natasha Hawkins (Lisa) and Cordelia Tullous (Nene)."

SUE SYLVESTER & CHEERIOS

Mary Beth Hutchins of Arlington, Va., writes: "My coworkers and friends Allison and Megan and I decided to go as our favorite Glee characters, donning our outfits to work. I still had my soccer warm-up from high school and Allison worked some magic to add "WMHS" to cheerleading costumes Megan found online."

SNOOKI

Elizabeth Tassone of Farmingville, N.Y., writes: "I just straightened my hair, poofed it up and wore lots and lots of bronzer! I had the rest of the outfit in my closet and completed the look with some pickle juice!"

RUSSELL BRAND & KATY PERRY

Keri Taylor of Livingston, La., writes: "I LOVE Katy Perry so my boyfriend and I decided we would be Katy Perry and Russell Brand and I think this pretty much nails it!"

LADY GAGA

Christine Stirrat of Westfield, N.J., writes: "I'm Lady Gaga. I am a huge fan of hers and was so excited to be her for Halloween. I even made my bag and glasses."

KRIS JENNER

Susie Montano of Tucson, Ariz., writes: "My mother-in-law Ana Davila dressed up as Kris Jenner. She even cut her hair for the costume."

OZZY & SHARON OSBOURNE

Heidi Weidmann of Midland, Texas, writes: "This is a picture of my parents Bob and Karen Weidmann from last Halloween. Us kids thought it would be way funny to have my dad dress up as Ozzy Osbourne because he's the total opposite of him. My mom was way interested in it and actually dyed her blonde hair that pretty red."

BRET MICHAELS

Shannon Michaels of Milton, Wis., writes: "My wife made me up for a Poison concert in 2009 as a joke for some friends. Well it did not turn out to be a joke when I had people chasing me down for pics and autographs. The clincher to this was when Bret Michaels requested to meet me and when I met him on his bus, he said, 'Holy Crap, there is my twin but you are better looking.'"

SPICE GIRLS

Christina Fontana of Baltimore, Md., writes: "My girlfriends and I, sophomore year of college, really wanted to be a group. There have always been five of us and we always joked we could be the Spice Girls, so we finally got our acts together and spiced up our Halloween that year."

BEETLEJUICE

Cassie Maher of Wellington, Fla., writes: "I got my boyfriend's stuff off eBay and I made my costume by hand. People kept stopping us and taking our picture. A lot of hard work but totally worth it."

GENE SIMMONS

Needless to say, I definitely stole the show in typical Lambert fashion."
