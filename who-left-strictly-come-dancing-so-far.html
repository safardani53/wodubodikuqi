Who left Strictly Come Dancing? Charles Venn and Karen Clifton exit the dance floor

Publish date: 2024-06-10

STRICTLY Come Dancing has waved goodbye to Charles Venn and Karen Clifton.

Here's the latest on who's left the show and who's still in the running for the Glitterball trophy.

Who left Strictly Come Dancing last night?

On December 2, 2018, Charles Venn and Karen Clifton became the tenth couple to leave Strictly after they found themselves in the bottom two.

They performed their rumba again in the dance-off against Pussycat Doll Ashley Roberts and Pasha Kovalav, who were in the bottom two for the second week running.

The judges chose to save Ashley as she performed a more accomplished and "technical" quickstep.

What time is Strictly Come Dancing 2018 on TV?

Strictly Come Dancing is on Saturdays at around 7pm on BBC One.

The results show goes out on Sunday evening at around 7.20pm.

Judges Shirley Ballas, Bruno Tonioli, Craig Revel-Horwood, and Darcey Bussell have returned to the show for another year.

Claudia Winkleman replaced the late Sir Bruce Forsyth in 2014, joining Tess Daly as the presenting duo.

Strictly: It Takes Two, hosted by Zoe Ball, is on BBC every weekday at 6.30pm, with an extended show starting at 6pm on Fridays.

The Strictly final is on December 15.
