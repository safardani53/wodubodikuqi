Jugal Hansraj is an award-winning Indian actor, model, producer, writer, writer, and director based in New York City. Hansraj began his career as a child actor in the 1982 film Masoom, starring Naseeruddin Shah and Shabana Azmi. He continued his career as a child actor in films such as Karma and Sultanate. He modeled for television and the press as a child and appeared in notable advertising campaigns. He played adult roles for the first time in Aa Gale Lag Jaa in 1994. He then starred in Mohabbatein, Kabhi Khushi Kabhie Gham and Salaam Namaste. He is the screenwriter and director for the 2008 computer animated film Roadside Romeo
