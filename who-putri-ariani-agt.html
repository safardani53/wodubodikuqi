Who is AGT's Putri Ariani?

Publish date: 2024-03-22

FORMER America's Got Talent contestant Putri Ariani is no stranger to the franchise: She competed in Indonesia's Got Talent in 2014.

She made her season 18 America's Got Talent debut with her rendition of U2's I Still Haven't Found What I'm Looking For, which earned her a Golden Buzzer.

Who is AGT's Putri Ariani?

Putri Ariani is a singer born on December 31, 2005, in Kampar, Riau, Indonesia.

Putri went viral after her audition on America's Got Talent which earned her a Golden Buzzer from Simon Cowell.

Putri made her way through the competition show but lost in the final episode to Adrian Stoica and Hurricane.

Apart from her journey with AGT, Putri also has her own music.

In 2020, Putri released her debut album called Melihat Dengan Hati.

Her 2022 song Tak Mampu Lupa went viral on Spotify, earning over 11million streams throughout the music service.

Was Putri Ariani born visually impaired?

Yes, Putri was born visually impaired.

During a TEDx performance, Putri spoke on her ability to persist no matter how she was born.

The Indonesian singer said: "I will never give up because I know that I'm invincible, not invisible.

"I'm capable. I'm powerful. I'm brave. I'm spectacular. I believe that everyone has their own potential."

Did Putri Ariani win Indonesia's Got Talent?

Putri Ariani won season two of Indonesia's Got Talent when she was only eight.

After her win, Putri was nominated for Best New Artist at the Indonesian Music Awards.

In 2010, Putri auditioned for The Voice Kids Indonesia.

Her success on The Voice landed her a spot as a finalist; unfortunately, she didn't win the competition.
