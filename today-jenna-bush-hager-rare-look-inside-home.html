Todays Jenna Bush Hager shares rare look inside stunning home featuring cozy fireplace and huge C

Publish date: 2024-07-25

JENNA Bush Hager has shown her followers what her home currently looks like for the holidays.

The Today host incorporated both Thanksgiving and Christmas into the decor.

On Sunday, Jenna, 41, posted multiple photos to her Instagram Stories.

The first picture featured her three children - Hal, 4, Poppy, 8, and Mila, 10 - sitting at their kitchen counter.

She shares her kids with her husband, Henry Chase Hager.

Mila wore a turkey hat on her head as she dipped a snack into her drink.

She and Hal looked back at the camera as Poppy paid attention to what she was eating.

Jenna then showed off other fall decor including a bowl of oranges and pinecones, sitting on a pinecone base.

The mother of three also photographed two fall wreaths - one with pinecones and one with oranges with pinecone edges in it.

Both were hung on top of mirrors and had a fall-themed centerpiece under them.

'TOO EARLY?'

Jenna also showed off her Christmas decorations, which she decided to put up early.

Mila and Poppy sat near the fireplace in their living room with their dog.

The fall decor now had Christmas lights threaded throughout it.

"Too early?" Jenna captioned the picture.

The next Story was of the Hager family's Christmas tree.

"We did it!" her caption read.

The tree featured blue tree balls, Santa Claus figurines, pictures of her children, chunky white beads, and more.

This is not the first time Jenna has shown her followers her home.

In April, she videoed her bedroom and shared what she thinks makes the room look beautiful.

Jenna admitted that her “time in bed is so precious," as she juggled co-hosting the morning show, holding book clubs, and running her production company.

She sat on the plush bedding in her room in blue pants and a half-sleeve, button-up blouse as she talked about her sponsored partnership with the brand Boll & Branch.

“My alarm clock goes off way, way too early,” Jenna said in the video.

“So, my time in bed is a commodity.”

She went on to say that she loves using Boll & Branch's sheets in all of the bedrooms of her home because "they are so soft."

The bed also featured textured white sheets and pillowcases, as well as a pale yellow and blue floral headboard.

Gray curtains were closed behind the furniture, while a small wooden nightstand could be seen on the side of the bed.
