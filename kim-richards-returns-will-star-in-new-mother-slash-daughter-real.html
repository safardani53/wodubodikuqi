Kim Richards Returns! Will Star in New Mother/Daughter Reality Show

Publish date: 2024-06-17

If you miss Kim Richards on The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills, don’t despair.

The socialite and her daughter Kimberly Jackson are set to appear alongside five other mother/daughter pairs in Lifetime’s new reality series, "The Mother/Daughter Experiment: Celebrity Edition."

The show will showcase the women as they receive guidance from a therapist in an "attempt to repair their broken relationships by airing all their dirty laundry," as reported by E! News.

Well, that sounds healthy. For us. Because laughter is the best medicine.

The other five mother/daughter pairs include:

  • Heidi Pratt (nee Montag) and mother Darlene Egelhoff
  • Courtney Stodden and mom Krista
  • Natalie Nunn of Bad Girls’ Club and mother Karen
  • Shar Jackson (Kevin Federline’s baby mama) and daughter Cassie
  • Hollywood Exes star Jessica Canseco and daughter Jessie

If the trailer below is any indication, viewers will be treated to a whirlwind of crying, yelling, pushing and gesticulating befitting a Lifetime show, including emotional lines like "you ripped my heart out of my soul" and battle cries such as "you guys are gonna see a mother/daugher have a f**king fight!"

But by the end, we will also see hugs, tears, declarations of forgiveness and, of course, mothers and daughters sitting together and looking off into the sunset.

It was a tumultuous year for Richards, who has been arrested twice, fired from RHOBH and suffered the loss of her husband Monty Brinson earlier this week.

Luckily, she appears to have been refraining from drugs and alcohol, and completed 60 days of sobriety in December.

The series will premiere March 1 on Lifetime.
