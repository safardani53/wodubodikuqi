‘KUWTK’: Bruce Jenner’s High Heels — Wears Louboutins

Khloe thoughtfully picks out and wraps four pairs of gorgeous high heels — size 13M — for Bruce to help him with his transition into womanhood. “I got you some pressies,” she says before handing over the silver-wrapped boxes.

After he opens them up, Bruce tries on a pair of Christian Louboutin heels, which is a favorite of Khloe and her sisters. Looks like he’ll fit in just fine with their fashion taste!

“I don’t know her taste yet, so I’m trying to figure that out,”Khloe says shyly as she hands over the presents to her stepdad.

“Well, to be honest with you, I don’t know her taste either,” Bruce admits. “But do you know how nice this is? Without even opening them, do you know how nice this is and how much I appreciate this?”

“Well, good,” Khloe says. “You deserve it.”

Bruce is super touched by her gesture, and tells her that he loves and appreciates the shoes. Then, Khloe jokes about “who wore it better”, and playfully tells Bruce that he’d better not steal her style!
