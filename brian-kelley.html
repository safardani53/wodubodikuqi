Brian Kelley Celebrity Profile Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-03-30

Bio:
Brian Kelley (born Brian Edward Kelley on August 26, 1985 in Ormond Beach, Florida), is one half of the Nashville-based country duo Florida Georgia Line. Brian met Tyler Hubbard at an on-campus church worship meeting at Belmont University in 2007, and from there they started playing sold out gigs. They recorded a successful first EP, Anything Like Me, in 2010, and were discovered by a producer shortly afterward at a county fair. He signed them to Big Machine. They released their first album, Here’s to the Good Times, in 2012, which included the remix of their hit single “Cruise,” featuring Nelly. Their second album, Anything Goes, was released in 2014.

Best Known For:
Brian is best known as one of the members of Florida Georgia Line.

Personal Life:
Brian married Brittney Cole on December 16, 2013. Prior to getting involved in the music industry, Brian was actually being scouted for major league baseball. While in high school, he was drafted by the Florida Marlins, but bowed out when he spent more time benched than playing. With Tyler, he owns a music publishing company called Tree Vibez Music.
