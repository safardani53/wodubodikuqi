Starbucks customers can get 50% off cold drinks today but you need to watch the clock

Publish date: 2024-09-21

COFFEE drinkers can score half off drinks from Starbucks, but only in the afternoon.

Every Wednesday from July 26 until August 9, Starbucks Rewards members can grab cold drinks for 50 percent off.

The WinsDays discount starts after 12pm and is valid for the next three Wednesdays.

Just note that customers may only grab one cold beverage per day.

Customers may not combine the deal with other offers or discounts.

Lastly, the half-off drinks are not available through Starbucks Delivers on Uber Eats or DoorDash.

STARBUCKS REWARDS

The WinsDays is only available to rewards members but those who have yet to sign up can do so for free online or in the app.

Members may redeem their Stars for merchandise, food, drinks, and add-ons like extra espresso.

Plus, members will receive other perks like complimentary drinks or food on their birthday and even free drip coffee refills while in the cafe.

To sign up, head to the Starbucks website and click join now.

Customers earn one Star per $1 spent when scanning their member barcode in the app, then pay with cash, credit/debit cards, or mobile wallets at participating stores. 

They can also earn one Star per $1 spent when linking a payment method and paying directly through the app.

However, customers will earn Stars twice as fast if they add money to their digital Starbucks Card using any payment option. 

Users will then scan and pay in one step or order ahead in the app.

MORE ON REWARDS

The Stars customers collect toward Rewards will expire after six months. 

However, the coffee giant will apply the oldest Stars toward any Rewards customers redeem regardless of the tier.

For example, if you earn 10 Stars on June 16 and 15 Stars on June 24, the Stars earned on June 16 will be used first when you redeem Stars for a Reward. 

To check your Stars, customers can view the expiring date in the Rewards section when logged into your account.
