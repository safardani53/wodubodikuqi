Publish date: 2024-09-07

DOLLAR Tree shoppers already spotted Valentine's Day decorations and gifts on shelves.

While we haven't gotten to 2024 yet, the discount store is already stocking up for February.

TikToker Meri (@dltree_target_ross_finds) shared a video of some of the new Valentine's merchandise that is currently on shelves.

The entire store was dripping in pink, white, and red heart-shaped goodies.

There were red jars shaped like teddy bears and a cute floral decoration with a sparkly bear on it as well.

She also found plastic storage baskets with little heart cut-outs in both red and pink.

Fans of gnomes have a ton of options, including small red or pink ornaments, two types of plush toys, and some tabletop gnomes holding love letters.

Make sure your neighbors feel the love with different yard picks that look like conversation heart candies or maybe a cute dinosaur holding a glittery Valentine.

Or decorate the coffee table with a mini Valentine's Day tree adorned with sparkly ornaments.

Dollar Tree has listed some of its seasonal goods on its website, featuring an assortment of candies, drinkware, and even festive socks.

In the comments of the video, fans expressed their excitement over the new items.

"They get me every damn year," wrote one person.

"I'm ready for you Dollar Tree!"

Another person added: "The date night jar is everything! I grabbed one last night."

AN UNREAL DEAL

Fellow TikToker Dee (@deecleans222) revealed a huge money-saving Dollar Tree secret.

"Do you love those Downy scent beads that normally go for $15 to $20 a bottle?" she asked in a video.

She claimed that the Dollar Tree Aroma Boosters are actually the same product that was made improperly.

Rather than looking like a bead, they have a flat side and look like a dome.

However, they go for just $1.25.

 "I thought this Dollar Tree urban legend was a myth! Well, I was pleasantly mistaken!" she said.

"Tell me that's not a steal. So if I were you I would run over to your nearest Dollar Tree because when they get them in stock they do go fast. I'd advise you to stock up."

Dee said she bought five for herself, and one commenter joked that she was the reason they could never find them in stock.

Another viewer wrote: "I'm obsessed with these and they work better than the name brand," exclaimed one impressed follower."
