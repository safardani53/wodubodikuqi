I bought a 'purebred' Yorkshire Terrier for 1,600 - but now he looks like a GOAT

Publish date: 2024-05-07

A DOG owner revealed how she bought a "purebred" puppy for £1,600 - but now he looks like a goat.

Fai Naomi took to TikTok to show her followers how her Yorkshire Terrier bizarrely came to resemble a billy goat.

The dog-mum, known by her followers as @fainaomi, posted the hilarious video captioned: "I bought a $2000 "pure" breed yorkie just for him to turn into..."

The nine-second video begins with a cute clip of the dark haired puppy in a carry case before cutting to a snap of him donning a white t-shirt.

The fluffy pooch is then shown on a walk as Fai's fans hearts were left melting at the adorable clip.

But nothing could prepare her followers for the bizarre change in her pup's appearance.

The TikTok post racked up more than 5,000 likes and over 3,000 comments from people who were baffled and amused by her poor pups look.
