Devastated Gemma Collins forced to miss parents 42nd wedding anniversary amid coronavirus lockdow

Publish date: 2024-03-31


A DEVASTATED Gemma Collins has been forced to wish her parents a happy 42nd wedding anniversary from afar amid the coronavirus lockdown.

The TOWIE star posted a loving message to her social media as she had no choice but to spend the day away from them.

She wrote: "42 YEARS MARRIED today HAPPY ANNIVERSARY to the best parents anyone could ever ask for

"you have given us the best life and showed us what love and marriage is about and that is RESPECT

"for richer for poorer in sickness and in health

"we love you so much and THANK YOU for teaching us everything you have in life and that's to WORK HARD and APPRECIATE everything .... in recent events re the media please spend more time with your family's (sic)".

The 39-year-old's words were accompanied by two pictures - one of the happy couple kissing and another of them with their famous daughter.

She added in another post: "see you once all this blows over".

Gemma recently revealed her 65-year-old mum Joan was in recovery after battling pneumonia in hospital.

Last week the reality show favourite shared a photo of her mum's hand tied up to a drip in the hospital.

Gemma wrote: "Thank you to all the nurses who helped my mum she's had pneumonia and on the mend NHS thank you thank you so over stretched right now my mum has rheumatoid arthritis and is high risk to infection."

The condition has Joan in and out of the hospital, and she also has to use a wheelchair to get around.
