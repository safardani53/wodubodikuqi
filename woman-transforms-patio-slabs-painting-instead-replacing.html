I transformed my garden slabs instead of getting new ones &amp; loved the results

Publish date: 2024-07-02

IT can be an expensive job maintaining a garden.

So when faced with the prospect of having to replace all the paving slabs in their garden, Emily and her family decided to go down another route - by painting the slabs.

"We decided to paint our slabs instead of getting new ones and this is how it went," she wrote over a video which showed the transformation.

From grimy slabs covered in dirt, they cleaned them up and painted them a dove grey colour - which gave the patio a chic, modern feel.

In a pinned comment on the video, Emily added: "We know it’s not going to last long term but we love it for now - we can always touch up or get new slabs in future."

She also revealed she'd used the Exterior masonry paint from Wilko for the job - £16 for five litres.
