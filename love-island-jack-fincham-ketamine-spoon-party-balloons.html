JACK Fincham posted on social media pictures of him with his brother Oliver wearing a ketamine spoon and inhaling a party balloon before going on Love Island.

The reality star, who last night admitted to using coke in "one silly lapse of judgement", brazenly said film Blow, which stars Johnny Depp as a drug pusher, was “inspirational”.

And in other picture on Instagram a pal of Jack's made comments referring to cannabis.

One post was captioned: “#Tbt summer can’t wait to be here again” shows him in Ibiza with a bikini-clad woman and his brother.

A friend, called dannyaker_1, observed: “Just clocked your brother’s k spoon. #Essential.”

A k spoon or ketamine spoon is used by drug takers to snort the powder.

In another he is seen inhaling a party balloon from with a wide-eyed pal while one other picture shows him next to a pal holding a balloon used to take the legal high.

Jack, 26, posted a picture of Johnny Depp in the movie Blow in which he plays George Jung who was an American cocaine smuggler.

He commented on the still calling it “the best ain’t it” before adding “#inspirational”.

In other snaps his brother Oliver comments “good kush” — another term for cannabis.

His brother also says “lidded up from early” — meaning to be extremely high off of marijuana — in a snap of Jack with a pal “raving”.

The posts were before the Love Island star admitted to The Sun he had binged on coke following his success on the ITV2 show last summer.

Jack revealed he caved in to temptation after an all-night “blowout” last Saturday.

He had been knocking back booze until the early hours at a club with pals, including fellow Love Island stars Adam Collard and Sam Bird.

The boyfriend of Dani Dyer, the daughter of EastEnders star Danny, blamed his fame rising dramatically after the the ITV2 show as the reason for taking the drugs.

He said: “I’m in a new world of partying and stuff, and being in circles that I would never normally have been in.
