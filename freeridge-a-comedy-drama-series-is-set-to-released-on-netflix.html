Freeridge A Comedy-Drama Series Is Set To Released On Netflix

Publish date: 2024-09-25

The new comedy-drama series Freeridge will release on Netflix on Thursday, February 2, 2023, at 3.01 a.m. ET. The program is a spinoff of On My Block, a famous Netflix adolescent comedy series. It follows a group of pals as they attempt to erase a potentially fatal curse. Bryana Salez, Keyla Monterroso Mejia, and many more have pivotal parts in the production. Lauren Iungerich, Eddie Gonzalez, Jeremy Haft, Jamie Uyeshiro, and Jamie Dooner direct Freeridge.

The trailer for Freeridge retains a unique and adventurous tone.

Netflix published the official trailer for Freeridge on January 6, 2023. It provides insight into the lives of the four main protagonists, who embark on an epic journey after they may have unwittingly unleashed a dreadful curse that might devastate their lives. One of the key characters says in the trailer:

“So we purchased this cursed box. And now we’re cursed as a result of it.”

The trailer successfully establishes the plot’s central point while avoiding big spoilers that may derail viewers’ enjoyment. Overall, the teaser retains a unique and adventurous tone that young comedy lovers will undoubtedly like. According to Netflix’s Tudum, here’s a brief synopsis of the upcoming series:

“Gloria (Keyla Monterroso Mejia), Ines (Bryana Salaz), Demi (Ciara Riley Wilson), and Cam (Tenzing Norgay Trainor) are your new Core Four, sure to fall into a mind-bending mystery — and, maybe, a little puppy love — when they come upon a box that may or may not be cursed.”

According to the official description and teaser for Freeridge, viewers can expect a funny comedy series that follows the adventures of four close friends who unwittingly set off a curse that seems to have come back to haunt them. The series will supposedly include eight episodes, all of which will be released on Netflix on the same day, February 2, 2023.

A short glance at the cast of Freeridge

Keyla Monterroso Mejia plays Gloria, the group’s dynamic and seductive leader, in one of the primary roles in the comedy series. She is characterized as “unapologetically herself,” and she is incredibly educated and sophisticated. Keyla looks fantastic in the part, effortlessly embodying her character’s essential characteristics. Viewers may expect a totally outstanding performance from the very skilled actress.

Keyla Monterroso Meijo is most recognized for her roles in HBO’s Curb Your Enthusiasm, Abbott Elementary, and The Estate, among others. Bryana Salaz, who plays the part of Ines, co-stars with Keyla in another crucial role. Ines is regarded to be the group’s least emotionally mature member. Bryana appears as great in the clip, slipping into her character with ease.

Other prominent film and television acting roles for Bryana Salaz include Team Kayile, Malibu Rescue, and many more. Ciara Riley Wilson, Tenzing Norgay Trainor, and many more round out the cast. Remember that all eight episodes of Freeridge will be available on Netflix on Thursday, February 2, 2023.
