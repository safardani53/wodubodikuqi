Mums rushing to Morrisons as pricey nappies slashed to a fiver... but you'll have to be quick

Publish date: 2024-06-03

MUMS are rushing to Morrisons after finding out the supermarket has slashed Pampers nappies by more than half-price.

But that’s just the start of the bargains. 

The deal is part of Morrisons’ Baby & Toddler Event which happens twice a year - once at the start and again in summer.

Pampers pull-ups have been reduced to £5 from £11 and contain up to 38 nappies per pack.

Shopper Eliza Todd took to Facebook Group Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK to reveal what she found in-store on the first day of the shopping event.

Pictures taken inside show a full display of nappies, with some parents moaning they splurged on a nappy stock-up before the deals kicked in.

“The Baby Event would start when I just stocked up on nappies on Friday,” one mum moaned. 

“I paid £11 for these last night,” another penned. 

Some mums warned that the nappies are the first thing to sell out every time Morrisons' Baby & Toddler Event is on.

Jumbo packs of Pampers nappies have also been slashed to £8.50 from £13.

Other bargains are three packs of Nutmeg baby wipes for £2.20, Fairy Outdoorable Fabric Conditioner 55 Washes for £3.49 and five for £4.50 on SMA PRO Growing Up Baby Milk Liquid Ready To Feed.

Johnson’s products have also been upgraded as a BOGOF deal. 

This includes everything from its iconic baby powder and baby oil to wash cloths and haircare products.

A number of food items have also been reduced. 

These include Kiddylicious baby snacks, Ella’s Kitchen Melty Sticks and baby food pouches.

Heinz pouches are on offer at three for £3, despite usually costing £1.15 a pop. 

Similarly, Heinz baby food jars - including custard, oat porridge and cheesy tomato pasta - are now five tubs for £3.50.
