An Unexpected Elimination Shocks The Final 5 Castaways On Survivor: Kaoh Rong

Publish date: 2024-06-28
After the castaways launched bags of puzzle pieces into the open targets across the way, they were knocked out of the game one-by-one when their bags missed the mark. However, Joe took his time and precisely landed his third and final bag into the target, which gave him the win and a Reward full of massages, baths, food, and much needed rest.

He chose Aubry to join him, then Aubry chose Cydney as the third guest, and off the trio went for the ultimate spa day.

Who would've thought that Joe, Survivor: Kaoh Rong's elder statesman at 71 years old, would snag one of the sweetest Rewards of the season?
