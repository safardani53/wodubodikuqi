Kourtney Kardashian wears blonde wig &amp; tights as she poses as True Romance's Alabama Worley for Hall

Publish date: 2024-06-21

KOURTNEY Kardashian transformed into True Romance's Alabama Worley for Halloween in a new photoshoot.

The reality star channeled the character alongside her fiancé Travis Barker who played the role of Clarence.

Kourtney gave her followers a close-up look at the transformation on Instagram sporting a short blonde wig and pink cheetah-print leggings.

She shared a series of pics donning the get-up which included a crab-themed light blue off-the-shoulder top.

She accessorized the ensemble with large round blue earrings and a matching oversized belt.

In the snaps, the model is sitting in her makeup chair gazing away from the camera.

Kourtney flashed her sparkly diamond engagement ring at the lens in one photo with her hand on her belt buckle, while in the others she is holding sunglasses.

The Poosh founder simply captioned the post: "Alabama Worley."

Her rocker beau, Travis, 45, also slid in a comment writing: "Our sons name would be Elvis," a nod to his portrayal of Clarence who was an Elvis-fanatic.

TRUE ROMANCE

On Sunday, Kourtney shared clips from the photoshoot on her Instagram Stories in honor of Halloween.

The 42-year-old shared behind-the-scenes snaps of the pair dressed up like Christian Slater and Patricia Arquette's characters in the movie.

Travis played his part wearing a red floral button down, blue jeans, a black slicked back wig and sunglasses.

In the clip, the Keeping Up With The Kardashians alum sat on the trunk of a convertible while the musician had his hands on her high heels, planting his lips on her foot. 

Additional photos showed the lovebirds making out on the hood of the car, in a phone booth, as well as cruising around in the vehicle together.

The film portrayed the lives of Clarence and Alabama who were star-crossed lovers on the run from the mob after stealing a suitcase of cocaine.

The inspiration behind the photoshoot came from the Blink-182 drummer's love for the 1993 film.

Travis famously named his 15-year-old daughter after Patricia's character. 

He is also dad to 17-year-old son Landon, both whom he shares with his ex Shanna Moakler.

Meanwhile, Kourtney is mom to Mason, 11, Penelope, nine, and Reign, six with her ex Scott Disick.

COUPLES COSTUME

The week prior, Kourtney and Travis mimicked another famous couple for a Halloween-themed shoot.

In the pics, Travis dressed up as Sex Pistols frontman Sid Vicious while Kourtney naturally played the part of his girlfriend Nancy Spungeon.

Kourtney wore a blonde, wavy wig for the costume, while Travis wore a dark wig in spikes.

The reality star posted photos of herself and her beau in costume on Instagram. She captioned the post: "till death do us part."

Travis commented back: "our love will never die"
