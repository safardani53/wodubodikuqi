Publish date: 2024-10-01

Credit: Perry Knotts/Getty Images

Brittany Mahomes

Brittany and her NFL quarterback husband, Patrick, have been “huge fans” of Swift for a while, attending the singer’s Kansas City Eras Tour stop in July 2023, a source exclusively told Us in October 2023.

Brittany was first seen with Swift in September 2023 during a girls’ dinner in New York City when the team was in town to play the New York Jets. They have since been spotted cheering together from the organization’s suite.

“The fact that Patrick and Travis are best friends makes things even better,” the insider told Us at the time. “They’re having a blast and love cheering on their men together.” Brittany, for her part, married Patrick in March 2022. The couple share daughter Sterling and son Bronze.
