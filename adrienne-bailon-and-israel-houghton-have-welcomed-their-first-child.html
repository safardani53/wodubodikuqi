Adrienne Bailon and Israel Houghton Have Welcomed Their First Child

Publish date: 2024-04-05

Adrienne Bailon and Israel Houghton just become the parents of a surrogate child. The singer announced the news on social media, writing,

“Always James / We prayed for this youngster. To hear our kid scream / Skin to skin, face to face / Heart to heart, eye to eye…”

Bailon said in the wonderful post that everyone is aware of their love story and the journey they went through before the baby’s arrival. She said that they had been silently praying for their baby’s safe arrival for the last nine months.

Bailon finished by saying:

“He is worth every cry, disappointment, delayed player, IVF cycle, and miscarriage.” Everything. We are overcome with pleasure, love, and thankfulness. We are grateful to God, our angel surrogate, and all of our friends and family who have supported us on this journey for over 5 years. He’s here, and we’ve never been happier to be awake! #HappilyEverHoughton”

All about Adrienne Bailon’s marriage.

Adrienne Bailon’s spouse, Israel Houghton, was born on May 19, 1971. For his ability to instill zest and energy in modern gospel and worship, the singer, composer, and producer is regarded as one of the most significant Christian music musicians at the time. In 1989, Israel chose to dedicate his time to the Worship Ministry, and in 1995, he created the music ensemble Israel & New Breed. The group’s first album, New Season, was released in 2001, followed by Real in 2002 and Live from Another Level in 2004. Houghton produced Michael Gungor’s debut album, Bigger than My Imagination, which was released in 2003. In 2006, he published Timeless Christmas, followed by A Deeper Level the following year.

The Power of One, Houghton’s debut solo album, was published in 2009, and the songs on the album were well appreciated by the audience. Love God, Love People, and London Sessions were his next two albums. In March 2012, he released a compilation album called Decade, followed by an album called Jesus at the Center in August 2012. He has received 11 Dove Awards, two Stellar Awards, and six Grammy Awards. Previously, the 51-year-old was married to gospel singer Meleasa Houghton. They married in 1994 and have four children together: Mariah, Milan, Jordan, and Israel II.
