Im a gardening lover - the exact plants to put in your borders so they look good all year round,

Publish date: 2024-06-27

DO you want to deck out your garden with plants and flowers but aren’t sure where to start?

A gardening lover has revealed the exact plants you should put in your borders so they look good all year round.

TikTok user @the_oakwood6 uploaded a video which has racked up over 6,000 likes thanks to her tips.

She shared how she often gets asked “what should I put in my border?” and revealed her reply.

First up, the gardening fan recommended adding “evergreen plants to keep the garden alive in winter.”

Her top recommendation for this was cordyline and blue fescue.

More on gardening

The gardening expert then suggested you add “plants you can shape to fit your garden.”

For this she said you could opt for bay trees and yew balls.

Finally, you should add “colour with seasonal plants and assorted alpines”, with camelia, rhododendron and azalea being top choices.

Many people were quick to take to the comments to give their views.
