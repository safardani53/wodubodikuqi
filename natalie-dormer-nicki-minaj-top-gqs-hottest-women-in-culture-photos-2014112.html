Natalie Dormer, Nicki Minaj Top GQ's Hottest Women in Culture: Photos

Publish date: 2024-09-01

Sexy ladies! 

With chart-topping singles, hashtag-worthy performances, and one fierce haircut, it’s no wonder that Natalie Dormer and Nicki Minaj made the cut of GQ’s Hottest Women in Culture list for 2014.

The curated list highlights unforgettable moments like Kendall Jenner and her beloved Instagram account, Jennifer Lawrence and her short-lived romance to Chris Martin, and Cara Delevingne’s enviable party-hopping, and more.   

Although she stars in the latest Hunger Games blockbuster installment, out now, Dormer earns the apt title of “Widow of the Year,” for her portrayal of Lady Margaery Tyrell on HBO’s Game of Thrones. Dressed in a cream sweater, gold sandals, and not much else, the mag sums up her marriage to King Joffrey, his sad demise, and how she eventually came to fall in love with his younger brother.
