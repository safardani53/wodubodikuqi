Southern Charm Star Austen Krolls Dating History: Photos

Publish date: 2024-06-21

Austen Kroll has bared it all during his time on Southern Charm — including taking fans inside his relationship highs and lows.

Kroll joined the cast of the Charleston-based series during season 4 in 2017. He has since given OG stars Shep Rose and Craig Conover a run for their money in the romance department.

“Austen has some pull on women. I don’t know,” Southern Charm alum Cameran Eubanks exclusively told Us Weekly in April 2022. “I mean, I’ll give it to the guy, he’s tall. He smells good. I don’t know what it is, but yeah, women love Austen.”

Kroll’s ability to woo the ladies has gotten him into hot water at times, including during his 2017 battle with Rose over costar Chelsea Meissner. Kroll’s messy romances continued with his on-off relationship with Madison LeCroy, whom he split from in 2020.
