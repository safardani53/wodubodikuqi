Jumanji: The Next Level when is the new movie out and is it part 2 or 3? The Sun

Publish date: 2024-09-07

THE highly-anticipated new film Jumanji: The Next Level is getting ready to hit UK cinemas and we can't wait.

For those who are just as excited to see the film as we are, read on to find information about the latest blockbuster movie.

When is Jumanji: The Next Level out?

The wait is almost over as Jumanji: The Next Level will grace our screens from Wednesday, December 11, 2019.

Several other countries have got the opportunity to see it earlier than the UK, as it arrived in places such as France and Finland on December 4.

America won't get the chance to see it until Friday, December 13.

You can watch the trailer above.

Who is in the cast of Jumanji: The Next Level?

The main characters from the first film are back and ready for new adventures in the new film - plus some new faces.

Here's who is in the star line-up:

  • Dwayne Johnson as Dr Smolder Bravestone - Eddie's avatar (who was previously Spencer's). Dwayne's character is a strong, confident archaeologist, explorer, and the leader of the team.
  • Danny DeVito as Eddie Gilpin - Danny plays Spencer's cranky, but well-meaning grandfather.
  • Jack Black as Professor Sheldon "Shelly" Oberon - Fridge's new avatar (who was previously Bethany's). He is a palaeontologist, archaeologist, cartographer, cryptographer, and geometric.
  • Ser'Darius Blain as Anthony "Fridge" Johnson - A Brantford High School football jock, who cares more about football than studying.
  • Kevin Hart as Franklin "Mouse" Finbar - Milo's avatar (who was previously Fridge's) Kevin's character is a diminutive zoologist, Linguistic and weapons specialist.
  • Danny Glover as Milo Walker - Eddie's eccentric friend.
  • Karen Gillan as Ruby Roundhouse - Martha's avatar who is a commando with talents in martial arts and is now able to wield nunchucks.
  • Morgan Turner as Martha Kaply - A shy and cynical intellectual at Brantford High School. She and Spencer started a romantic relationship at the end of Welcome to the Jungle.
  • Nick Jonas as Jefferson "Seaplane" McDonough - Nick plays a young aircraft pilot.

Is Jumanji: The Next Level the second or third part of the franchise?

This is the THIRD film in the Jumanji franchise.

The first film that was just called Jumanji was released in 1995.

Fans then had to wait 22 years for the next one when Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle was released in 2017.

The original film starred the late Robin Williams, however the reboot is headed up by Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson and Kevin Hart.

What happens in Jumanji: The Next Level?

In Jumanji: The Next Level, the gang is back but the game has changed.

As they return to rescue one of their own, the players will have to brave parts unknown from arid deserts to snowy mountains, to escape the world's most dangerous game.

Unknown to his friends, Spencer kept the pieces of the Jumanji video game and one day repaired the system in the basement of his grandfather's house.

When Spencer's friends Bethany, Fridge, and Martha arrive, they find Spencer missing and the game running and decide to re-enter Jumanji to save him.

Spencer's grandfather Eddie and his friend Milo Walker hear the commotion and inadvertently get sucked into the game too before any of Spencer's friends can select their avatars.

With a new quest offered to them by Nigel Billingsley, the teenaged friends must help Eddie and Milo get used to their in-game avatars, get them to help find Spencer and Bethany, and escape Jumanji once again and for all.
