Kacey Musgraves Transformation from Girl-Next-Door to Experimental Glam Queen Experts Weigh I

Publish date: 2024-09-06

Singer and songwriter Kacey Musgraves is celebrated for her musical compositions that have won her accolades and honors including six Grammys, seven Country Music Association Awards, and three Academy of Country Music Awards.

The Golden Hour composer's music is not the only reason she has been making headlines over the past few years— her plastic surgery has been the subject of media scrutiny. 

Although Musgraves has not publicly disclosed whether she has had plastic surgery, fans as well as some leading plastic surgeons believe she has been under the knife.

Kacey Musgraves's transformation pictures, before and after alleged plastic surgery. 

On October 15, 2020, Radar Online shared opinions from three plastic surgeons who believe the singer surgically enhanced her facial features. Take it into consideration that none of those surgeons have treated her.

One of the three surgeons, Anthony Youn, M.D., F.A.C.S., suspected Musgraves got a nose job. He said:

Her nose just looks thinner and more refined than it used to and the only way to achieve this is through rhinoplasty.

Dr. Youn further observed Masgraves' lips look different than before. “She may have also undergone lip fillers recently, causing her lips to look a lot more plump then they once were," he said.

Another expert, Dr. Dennis Schimpf, M.D., F.A.C.S., said the volume of Musgraves' face has a dramatic difference. Weight loss might have played a role, but Dr. Schimpf said volume reduction in the cheeks and neck has similar results.

“This can be achieved via injections of such products as Kybella, or surgically via excision of buccal fat or facial chin liposuction,” Dr. Schimpf explained.

He and Dr. Youn were on the same page about Musgraves’ lips. Dr. Schimpf said the Space Cowboy singer’s lips appear fuller and more defined.

Overall, the size of her nose and width of her nose is smaller, most likely due to a well-performed rhinoplasty.

Adam Schaffner, M.D., F.A.C.S., who is the Director of the Plastic Surgery Institute Of New York, also shared how Musgraves has had some touch-ups since rising to fame.

He said she might have used make-up, lasers, chemical peels, radiofrequency and/or great skin care products to get that luminous skin tone.
