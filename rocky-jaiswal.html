Rocky Jaiswal Biography

Rocky Jaiswal was born on February 14, 1989, as Jayant Jaiswal in Kolkata, West Bengal, India. He recently visited Mumbai, Maharashtra, India.

He enrolled in a private school in Kolkata for his primary education. According to reports, he graduated from a prestigious college with a degree in filmmaking and production. He is passionate about filmmaking and producing, so he moved away from his hometown to pursue his dream.

Jaiswal adores and admires his mother. Lata Jaiswal is his mother’s name, and she is a housewife. Before beginning any project, he always seeks his mother’s blessing. In Kolkata, his father owns a business.

He celebrates Raksha Bandhan with his three sisters, Nirja Jaiswal, Neelam Singh, and Ritu Jaiswal. Neelam is a blogger on YouTube. He practices Hinduism and belongs to the Kalwar caste.

Rocky Jaiswal Wife, Marriage

Regarding his love life, Rocky and Hina’s relationship is well-known, so let’s take a look at the couple’s love story. They first met on the set of the television show ‘Yeh Rishta Kya Kehlata Hai,’ in which Hina Khan was the lead actress and Rocky Jaiswal was the supervising producer.

During the show, they became friends, and their friendship eventually turned into love. Hina and Rocky collaborated for about eight years. The couple’s relationship was not revealed until 2017. Rocky then made a special appearance to meet Hina Khan while she was a contestant on the reality show ‘Fear Factor: Khatron Ke Khiladi.’

Furthermore, Hina admitted to being in a relationship with Rocky Jaiswal while competing on the reality TV show ‘Bigg Boss 11’. According to reports, Rocky proposed to Hina in 2018 in Dubai with a diamond ring, and she said ‘Yes.’

Career

Rocky Jaiswal abandoned his family business and home to pursue his dreams. He is the owner of a wedding planning business. Aside from that, he has launched an app called ‘Rockabyte,’ where people can showcase their talent.

As of June 2021, he has produced numerous TV shows and films. He is the co-owner and co-founder of the production company ‘Hiro’s Faar Better Films with Hina Khan. In 2010, he was the executive producer of ‘Agnipareeksha Jeevan Ki: Gangaa.’

He then went on to co-produce other TV shows like Sasural Simar Ka (2011), Kuch Toh Log Kahenge, Yeh Rishta Kya Kehlata Hai, Saath Nibhana Saathiya, and others. From 2009 to 2010, he worked as an assistant director on the film ‘Mitwa – Phool Kamal Ke.’

In 2020, he released a film called Wishlist, which was available on MX Player. Similarly, in 2020, he was a co-producer on the film Doorman.’ His film ‘Lines’ won the award for Best Feature Film Cinematography. In this film, Hina Khan plays the lead role.
