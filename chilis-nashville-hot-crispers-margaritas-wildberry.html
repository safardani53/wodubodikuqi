Chilis adds heat spin to fan favorite and launches 10 new items to celebrate the fall

Publish date: 2024-04-15

CHILI’S has made some major menu changes for the fall and customers have many new options to choose from.

The casual restaurant is putting a spicy kick on one of its most popular entrées and serving up 10 new creations at the bar.

Chili’s has made some significant menu changes in time for the fall and the new football season.

First up, the chain is adding a new flavor to its beloved Chicken Crispers lineup.

Available to order now, Nashville Hot has a distinctive, spicy kick and a smooth, oil-like texture, perfect for keeping those 100 percent white meat Crispers tender and tasty.

This flavor can also be added to boneless or bone-in wings.

A fan creation, this beverage is simply a Modelo topped off with a frozen margarita.

FALL FLAVOR

Chili’s has chosen Wildberry as its signature fall flavor.

This sweet and sour blend of berries can also be enjoyed in a new Presidente - a Cuban mixed drink starring rum and orange liqueur.

Wilberry is also available in three soft drinks:

  • Wildberry tea
  • Lemonade
  • Arnold Palmer

HAPPY TIMES

To get fans sipping on these new creations, Chili’s is extending its happy hour.

Customers can take advantage of margaritas from $6 any time college or pro football is on the TV.

For those who think that wings and football are meant to be together, Chili’s is also offering buy one, get one free on any eight-count order of wings placed on Monday.

As it turns out, Chili’s is not the only joint that thinks wings are a must-have during football season.
