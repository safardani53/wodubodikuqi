BTSs Jimins Stunning Aesthetics Visual Have Taken Over The Internet

Publish date: 2024-04-26

Jimin is the third member of the group to release his unique 8 Photo-Folio, after RM and Jung Kook. In the images, he is dressed in elegant black and white ensembles that showcase his great features. The photographs are captioned ID: Chaos, referencing the impact the Serendipity singer has on his followers’ hearts and thoughts.

BTS Jimin’s amazing images have taken over the internet.

With his new images, the Promise singer is sure to make waves on social media. Me, Myself, Jimin’s ID: Chaos’ Special 8 Photo-Folio is the title of his photofolio. Of course, ARMYs have reacted to the Filter singer’s new photographs on social media, and we adore it. Other fans turned the incident into a meme, with Jin having the “most relatable” reply to the Serendipity singer. Me, Myself, and Jimin, HYBE’s debut concept video for his picture portfolio, was published on September 26. The Promise singer exhibited his magnificent visuals in the concept video, displaying a peaceful vibe and his trademark duality in all its grandeur.

The BTS member picked the black and white hues in the previously published mood sampler because they reflect two opposing sides of the same coin. He also said in the video that he wants to offer people a fresh side of him that is still essentially him without any extra “ornaments.”

BTS Jimin’s Louvre picture will be displayed in his family’s Busan café.

A collection of artwork inspired by the Promise vocalist and created by the accomplished South Korean artist Lee K was shown in the Louvre museum in Paris and is currently proudly displayed at Magnate café in Busan, which is owned by the BTS member’s father. Artist Lee K personally hung the Filter singer’s photos at his family-owned café on September 22. For the uninitiated, his magnificent image was shown by artist Lee K inside the Carrousel du Louvre at this year’s Focus Art Fair, which ran from September 1 to 4.

Fans who were unable to attend Paris may now see the gorgeous image at his family-owned café in Busan. ARMYs also express how delighted the BTS member’s father must be to have his images displayed in the café. On October 13, the Promise vocalist will celebrate his birthday, and the band will play in Busan’s Asiad stadium to assist South Korea to win the bid for the “2030 World Expo.”
