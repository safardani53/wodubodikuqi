Mason Disick makes rare appearance in Kris Jenner's pics after getting snubbed by stepdad Travis Bar

Publish date: 2024-06-16

KOURTNEY Kardashian's eldest son Mason Disick is often hidden away from the family's limelight but has made a rare appearance.

Scott Disick's son celebrated his 14th birthday on Thursday alongside his brother Reign, 9, but was not acknowledged publicly by some family members.

Kris Jenner, grandmother to the two kids, commemorated the occasion by posting a series of photos on Instagram.

The social media post started with a photo of Mason lying on a couch with his sister Penelope Disick.

Penelope had her arms around her brother in the throwback photo.

They both wore pajamas and looked to be enjoying a family day on the leopard print couch.

Other photos of Mason included a selfie with Kris where he leaned over her shoulder and stuck his tongue out, which was completely black.

Kris also included a photo at a party where she wore a long-sleeved sparkly dress and had her arm around her grandson.

Mason looked smart and wore a white T-shirt with a blue blazer jacket on top.

He gave a small smile at the camera in a rarely-seen photo.

Kris captioned the photo: "Happy birthday to my two amazing grandsons, Mason and Reign!!!

'BLESSED'

"What are the chances of having two grandsons with the same exact birthday?!?!?! !! You are two of the loves of my heart and I am so blessed that God chose me to be your grandma!!!

"We have the most precious memories together and I cherish every single one. Mason you are truly so special, kind, sweet, thoughtful, smart, talented, creative and have the best skills on a dirt bike!!" she said.

She concluded: "Reign you are inquisitive, creative, loving, funny, talented, smart, sweet, and full of energy… I love you both with all my heart !!! @kourtneykardash @letthelordbewithyou."

SNUBBED?

Kris' loving post was one of several that came from the Kardashian family members for Mason.

However, Mason's stepdad Travis Barker, who married Kourtney in 2022, did not mark the occasion.

Khloe Kardashian, aunt to the boys, uploaded a heartfelt message.

"Happy birthday Mase," she wrote, "How in the world are you 14 already?! True, Tatum, and I love you so much!"

Kim Kardashian shared baby photos of both Mason and Reign.
