Kendall Jenner: No Makeup, All Freckles!

Publish date: 2024-08-21

Kendall Jenner without clothing? There is something we see on Kendall Jenner’s Instagram account nearly everyday.

But Kendall Jenner without makeup? That’s a sight as rarely seen as Kylie Jenner actually covering up her cleavage.

The 19-year old model ditched her typically glamorous appearance for a moment on Sunday in order to give fans a peek at what she looks like before the eye-liner, lipstick and blush take over her face.

What do you think of Kendall’s all-natural mug?

Rocking an impressive eyebrow game and some clear nail polish, Jenner simply captioned this picture with two emojis: a baby face and an alien face.

Because, we assume, a baby’s face also lacks makeup and because it’s an alien concept for Kendall to reveal herself in such bare fashion

Unless, again, we’re talking about bare naked Kendall Jenner. Folks have sadly seen that a lot of late.

Jenner, meanwhile, is the latest star to post a no makeup selfie, a gesture most celebrities make in order to help their fans with their self-esteem issues.
