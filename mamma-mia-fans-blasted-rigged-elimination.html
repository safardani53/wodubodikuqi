Mamma Mia! I Have A Dream sparks fix row as fans slam ITV judges for rigged elimination

Publish date: 2024-08-08

MAMMA Mia! I Have A Dream viewers have accused the show of 'rigging' the latest elimination.

The brand new singing competition continued on Sunday night as the remaining men and women battle it out.

Each contestant is competing for a place in the Mamma Mia musical in the iconic West End.

At the end of the series two hopefuls will win and walk away with the parts of Sophie and Sky.

However, another two singers were sent packing from the show and put back on a plane to the UK.

After each contestant performed for choreography week it was then up to judges Alan Carr, Jessie Ware and Amber Riley to deliver the result.

They announced that Zachkiel and Leah had lost their places in the competition.

But as host Zoe Ball comforted them on stage viewers at home were left raging by the result.

Taking to social media to comment, one fan said: "Desmonda in the bottom two and Leah going home. This is rigged idc justice for my girls."

A second wrote: "Judges are annoying me with Leah… saying is it too late? She has had nothing but positive comments! Imo they’re trying their hardest to get rid of her."

"Whatttttttt, what are the judges thinking sending Leah home," raged a third.

While a fourth posted: "Definitely disagree with the judges on their comments but yeah."

After her elimination, Leah said: "It's just something I have worked for my entire life and that I've always wanted.

"But no matter what I'm still proud of myself."

Mamma Mia! I Have A Dream continues next Sunday at 6pm on ITV1 and ITVX.
