Who is Rutshelle Guillaume:

Rutshelle Guillaume is a famous World Music Singer. She was born on July 28, 1988 and her birthplace is Port-au-Prince, Haiti. Rutshelle is also well known as, Singer who became a star in the Haitian music scene with solo songs like “Denye Won,” “Je Suis” and “Victorious.” She has been a frequent collaborator with other big name Haitian artists, including Roody Roodboy, Kai and Fantom. 

Rutshelle is originated from Haiti. Her album Emotions (2014) features Emeline Michel.
