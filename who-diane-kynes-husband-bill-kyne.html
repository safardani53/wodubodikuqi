Who is Diane Kyne's husband, Bill Kyne, and where is he now?

Publish date: 2024-05-11

FLORIDA murder case of Diane Kyne has yet to be resolved after over 20 years of investigation.

NBC's Dateline has made the 49-year-old's homicide the focus of their latest episode airing on July 29, 2022.

Who is Diane Kyne's husband, Bill Kyne?

William Edward Kyne, 63, made headlines back in 2010, after his wife, Diane Kyne, aged 49, was found murdered in their Seminole home.

Diane and Bill met in the early 2000s at the nail salon she ran with her twin sister, according to TheCinamaholic. At the time, Diane had a 15-year-old son, Kevin, from a previous marriage.

The pair tied the knot in 2002 and at the age of 19, Kevin changed his last name to Kyne.

On August 15, 2010, Diane reportedly died of strangulation or asphyxiation, according to the police.

Diane's husband and son were both present at the crime scene and pointed the blame at one another.

Kevin, however, was ultimately convicted of second-degree murder and sentenced to life in prison in 2012 after investigators found his broken glasses at the bottom of her bed and a few drops of his blood on his mother’s leg and bed cover.

However, two years later his lawyers appealed the conviction and he was given a new trial. Kevin was released from prison after being found not guilty.

The investigation remains in the air as it is still unclear as to who is guilty of Dinae's murder: Bill or Kevin.

Where is Bill Kyne now?

In 2018, Bill Kyne published a memoir, Love That Lasts A Lifetime.

"Through candid anecdotes and inspirational advice, Kyne invites all of us to see how with hope, love, and faith, we too can reach the highest mountain tops and survive the deepest valleys of despair," the book's description reads.

Bill currently resides around the Palm Harbor area and serves as a small business owner and investor.

In 2019, he was charged with leaving the scene of an accident with injury.

Authorities said he crashed his motorcycle into a group of cyclists, sending one to the hospital, according to the Tampa Bay Times. 

How many times has Bill Kyne been married?

Before tying the knot with Diane, Bill married Krista, his first wife.

Krista died in the same Seminole home he later shared with Diane, according to Oxygen.

"Investigators believe Krista tripped, hit her head and fell into the pool, where she drowned," the outlet reported.

"The death was ruled an accident and Bill collected approximately $250,000 from an insurance policy he had on his wife."

After Diane's death, Bill reportedly remarried a woman named, Tonya.

She, unfortunately, passed away in 2017 from cancer.
