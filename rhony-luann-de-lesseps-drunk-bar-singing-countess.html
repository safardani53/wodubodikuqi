RHONY star Luann De Lesseps 'kicked OUT of a gay piano bar' after a 'drunken onstage performance of

Publish date: 2024-08-11

RHONY star Luann De Lesseps has been ‘kicked OUT of a gay piano bar' after a 'drunken onstage performance of her own song’, according to PageSix.

A tipster told PageSix that Luann, also known as the Countess, was allegedly booted out of a New York piano bar after she reportedly hijacked the mic and yelled at hecklers.

According to one source, she kept “belting out jumbled versions of songs” in front of the audience who “began booing her.”

A video shows a seemingly drunken Luann at the microphone of Townhouse, a Manhattan piano bar, slurring her words and telling a booing patron “f**k you”.

She reportedly tried to sing her own song, Money Can’t Buy You Class, but the piano player accompanying her didn’t know it.

Another short clip appears to show her arguing with a member of staff.

The Countess denies being ejected from the venue, but a tipster told Page Six that she was “thrown out for getting aggressive with the bar’s customers.”

The alleged incident happened on Wednesday, and the following day Luann spoke to Page Six through an intermediary to explain.

“I got up and sang a few Broadway songs they were playing,” she said.

“I realize I was a little bit tipsy, but we were having fun, and it was getting late. I’d been rehearsing, and I was in the mood to sing a few songs. Then Jim [Kierstead] said to me, ‘Lu, I have an early rehearsal,’ and then we left around midnight, 11:30 p.m.”

ARREST SCANDAL

It’s not the first time that the Real Housewife’s antics have got her into a spot of bother.

She was arrested on December 23rd, 2017 in Palm Beach, Florida, and charged with battery on an officer and disorderly intoxication.

She pleaded guilty to three charges: battery, trespassing, and disorderly intoxication.

The reality star received one year of probation and was required to perform 50 community service hours, attend two AA meetings a week, and avoid drugs and alcohol.
