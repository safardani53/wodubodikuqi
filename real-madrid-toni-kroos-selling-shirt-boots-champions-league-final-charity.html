Real Madrid star Toni Kroos selling shirt and boots from Champions League final win in Kiev for char

Publish date: 2024-02-17

REAL MADRID star Toni Kroos is selling his match-worn shirt and boots from last weekend's Champions League final win for charity.

The selfless German star, 25, is aiming to raise £13,000 for his own foundation, with bids already reaching £9,000 for the memorabilia.

The Toni Kroos Foundation aims to help ill children and the player himself hopes his match-worn attire can go a long way to aiding them.

Real star Kroos tweeted: "Anyone interested in my matchworn @championsleague final boots and a prepared jersey from last saturday?"

The tweet came complete with a link to his charity page, which itself reads: "Toni Kroos succeeded in winning this year's Champions League against Liverpool. Historic.

"No German football player before him has managed to take the trophy four times.

"And for his fans, exceptional footballer Toni Kroos now has something completely unique from the finale ready...

"He is donating his original worn and even torn football boots and his match-worn jersey.
