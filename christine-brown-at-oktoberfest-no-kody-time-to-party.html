No Kody, Time to Party For Sister Wives Star!

Publish date: 2024-02-05

Kody Brown?

Kody Brown who?!?

This appears to be the reaction of all the reality star’s spouses these days, as nearly every one of the Sister Wives has stopped pretending.

They’re just traveling all over the place and doing really fun things — without their spiritual husband anywhere near their side.

The latest case in point would be Christine Brown.

The veteran TLC personality was all smiles over this past holiday weekend while attending Snowbird’s Oktoberfest with her family in her home state of Utah.

As you can see above, the mother of six spent the afternoon with her and Kody children Aspyn, Paedon, Mykelti and Truely, sharing a slideshow of portraits captured during the annual event on Sunday, September 5.

Mykelti also brought along her adorable five-month-old daughter, Avalon, for the outing.

“Fun with Family! #oma #momlife,” Christine captioned her snapshots.

Brown, of course, is a native of Utah.

She concluded the most recent season of Sister Wives by urging her loved ones to move back to their home state, admitting she felt lonely and lost in Arizona.

After getting rejected by Kody and company, Christine expressed her extreme frustration on air.

And also her extreme sadness.

"Meri, I can’t do marriage with Kody anymore," the 49-year old confessed to Kody’s first spouse, who told a tearful Christine to "look at the mountain."

"I don’t want to do it anymore," Christine reiterated, ignoring Meri’s plea for her to stop and appreciate her surroundings and adding:

"I don’t want to look at the mountains and remember the angels, how they were singing and I felt this pull.

"I agreed to move to Flagstaff and I followed Kody here, but it’s been a struggle the whole time."

The season basically ended with Christine saying the following:

"I’m tired of feeling like I don’t matter. I’m tired of not having his support when I really need it. I’m just tired.

"I need a partnership. I need something different than this."

In the time since this episode was filmed, Christine has not made any move.

She remains in Utah.

TLC, meanwhile, announced last week that Sister Wives will return with new episodes in November.

Based on the official synopsis, it sounds as if tension will remain between Christine and Kody, too.

Elsewhere, Meri Brown also took a trip late last week… also without Kody.

The star jetted all the way off to Cancun.

"I have arrived! Literally just got checked into my suite aka home for the next 6 days," wrote Meri as a caption of a selfie at her hotel.

"This week is a much needed break and rejuvenation for me. I’m looking forward to just being. Just existing. Just letting go."

Concluded Meri in her post:

"Not gonna lie, there’s been a few moments of anxiety leading up to this trip, and even a few moments today.

"It’s a bit strange traveling after all this time.

"But Cancun, I’m here for you, you’re here for me, it’s gonna be a good week!"
