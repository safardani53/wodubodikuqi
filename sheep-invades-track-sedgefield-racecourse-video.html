Watch hilarious moment SHEEP invades race track and manages to evade desperate attempts to capture i

Publish date: 2024-06-15

YOU can have a punt on almost anything at the races these days - but the likelihood of anyone backing a SHEEP to invade the track is very low.

But that is exactly what happened at Sedgefield Racecourse on Thursday.

The races were interrupted on Thursday after a daring sheep invaded the track and evaded a number of desperate attempts to capture it.

Sheepherders did try to ferry it towards the pen where it had come from, but after failing to find a way through the sheep turned back towards the track and continued its disruptive behaviour.

Fans online saw the funny side though.

"I don't baalieve it," said one Twitter user.

A second added: "Ewe must be joking!"

Others opted to highlight the sheep's racing potential.

One user wrote: "It's a shame it broke out the stalls, else it would have had a chance."

Another said: "They want to race :) why not allowing?"
