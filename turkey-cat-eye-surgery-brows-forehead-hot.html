A WOMAN who flew to Turkey for cat eye surgery has hit back at cruel trolls who say they dislike her new face.

Nasrin, who is a massive fan of plastic surgery, has had several procedures before - including numerous boob jobs, two rhinoplasties, filler in her chin, jawline and lips, and bum implants.

But most recently, the beauty fan shared a video where she could be seen asking her surgeon to explain his latest work.

"We performed temporal lifting for the brows and temple area, cat eye surgery to lift the corner of the eyes, and cheek lifting to lift the mid-face and cheeks," he explains in the clip posted to TikTok (@aestheticcliniclondon).

"We're performing these surgeries because of the sagging of the brows and the eyes that can occur in early age which can make people look tired and sleepy even if they're not."

Pointing to the skin next to Nasrin's eye, he says: "Lifting this part will improve the cheek fullness and also improve the definition of the mid-face and the jawline slightly, so it'll give a more V-shaped and defined face contour."
