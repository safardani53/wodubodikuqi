Diana Krall tour 2022: How can I buy tickets?

Publish date: 2024-04-03

DIANA Krall is a Canadian jazz pianist and singer who has sold over 15 million albums.

In 2022, she will be embarking on a worldwide tour, and now fans want to know how they can buy their tickets.

If you click on a link in this story we may earn affiliate revenue.

Diana Krall tour 2022: How can I buy tickets?

Tickets for Diana Krall's tour are on sale now and start at $49 plus taxes and fees.

They can be purchased online through Krall's official website or on Ticketmaster.

At this time, it remains unclear if VIP packages will be available.

What is Diana Krall's net worth?

Since the start of her career, Krall has released a dozen studio albums and three live records.

Due to her success, she has been able to amass an estimated net worth of $20million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

This number is also from various film and TV appearances.

Outside of music, Krall is known as the voice of Pete's Mom on the TV series Pete the Cat.

Krall has also been seen in At First Sight and Public Enemies.
