Jeremy Thomas Camp is a contemporary American Christian singer and songwriter from Lafayette, Indiana. Camp has released eleven albums, four of which are certified by the RIAA as Gold, and two live albums. His original music was a mix of ballads and fast paced songs with rock influences. Camp has won five GMA Dove Awards, was nominated for three American Music Awards, and was nominated for a Grammy Award for Best Contemporary Pop / Gospel Album of 2010 for his album, Speaking Louder Than Before.
