Elle King is a force to be reckoned with in the music industry. See photos of the talented singer here.

Elle King was born on July 3, 1989 in Los Angeles, California, to former Saturday Night Live cast member Rob Schneider and London King, a former model. At the age of 13, King started playing guitar, and was influenced by artists such as Otis Redding, the Yeah Yeah Yeahs, Etta James, Aretha Franklin, Al Green, Hank Williams, Johnny Cash, and AC/DC. By age 16, she started gigging around New York City, King was then signed by newly appointed RCA Chairman Peter Edge. Her debut single “Good To Be A Man”, was released on March 13, 2012. In September of 2014, the singer released “Ex’s & Oh’s”, which serves as the lead single off her debut album. She released the album, Love Stuff on February 17, 2015. She performed “Ex’s &amp; Oh’s” the next day on The Today Show to promote it. The song has reached number 10 on the Billboard Hot 100, becoming King’s first top 10 single in the United States!
